Becca Droz - The Amazing Race Cast Member

Age: 26
Hometown: Pittsburgh, Penn.
Current occupation: Rock Climbing Instructor and Barista.
Teammate: Floyd Pierce

Describe what you do: I facilitate all women’s climbing courses to help women become more confident and capable climbers and grow the community of lady climbers.

3 words to describe you: Fun, bold and adventurous.

Favorite hobbies: Beatboxing and freestyling, dancing (swing dance, contra dance and dancing at da club), juggling, skiing, gardening and video editing.

What is the accomplishment you are most proud of? Achieving my dream of being a contestant on The Amazing Race, duh.

What scares you most about traveling? Getting very lost.

What excites you most about traveling? New perspectives, new sights, new people and new experiences.

What qualities do you hope your partner has? Freestyling and/or beatboxing skills, a positive attitude, excels at puzzles and navigation, never gives up and likes to have fun!

What qualities will be hard for you to put up with in a partner? Negativity, drama, arrogance, too serious and anti-fun.

What do you think will be the biggest challenge you and your teammate face? Getting caught up in small moments and losing sight of the big picture.

What country and place would you most like to visit and why? New Zealand - Phil’s homeland and a place of great, mountainous beauty!

What do you hope to accomplish by running The Race (other than winning one million dollars)? Connect with a group of unique and fascinating individuals, push my limits and intimately experience new cultures.
