Kimora Lee Simmons See Pics Hollywood Life

Kimora Lee Simmons is a model, fashion designer and creator of the successful Baby Phat clothing line. The 6ft fashionista was married to music mogul Russell Simmons – the brother of Run DMC rapper Joseph “Rev Run” Simmons – from 1998 until 2009. Together, they have two daughters, Ming (born in 2000) and Aoki who was born two years later. Sadly the Simmons’ marriage didn’t survive, but Kimora went on to find love again with actor and model, Djimon Hounsou. The ‘Amistad’ actor is father to her third child, a son, Kenzo, who was born in 2009. Kimora went on to have a fourth child, another son, with her second husband Tim Leissner. Their little boy, Wolfe, was born in April 2015.

Kimora is well-connected in the showbiz world. Her high profile friends include supermodel Tyra Banks and the late Kim Porter, Diddy’s ex-girlfriend and the mother of four of his children. In 2019 Kimora revealed that she is relaunching her fashion line Baby Phat.
