Blake Shelton 'brags' about new $20k tractor on Oklahoma ranch after spending holiday away from wife

BLAKE Shelton has bragged about his expensive Christmas present in a new video after spending New Years away from his wife, Gwen Stefani.

The couple rang in the New Year apart, with Blake performing at Nashville's Big Bash and Gwen at The Party in Las Vegas.

Hours after his performance, Blake, 47, took to Instagram to gush over the $20,000 tractor he was gifted.

He posted a reel showing the vehicle at his Oklahoma ranch and shared his excitement about the present.

"I hate it when people brag about their Christmas gifts, but look what I got from Kubota!" the country singer yelled, pointing at the tractor.

Blake continued to boast about his gift as he gave his followers a closer look.

"I love it!" The Voice alum concluded.

He again shared his enthusiasm in his caption: "New year, new Kubota!!!!! Woohoo!!! Thanks y'all!!!!! #kubota #kubotacountry."

NOT SO LIVELY

The post came hours after Blake's buzzed-about New Year's Eve performance that many complained was not what they expected.

Blake took to the stage with Trace Adkins to perform Hillbilly Bone - a song they sang together back in 2011.

SEPARATE GIGS

Meanwhile, Gwen, 54, hosted a separate New Year's Eve party in Sin City, causing her to miss her husband's show.

"Well, it's funny because I was going to be at his show, and then Vegas called and wanted me to be in Vegas," the No Doubt alum previously told Access Hollywood.

"Last year I did Vegas and I was like, 'Remind me to never do Vegas again.' [But] you sometimes want to... not be the host.

"So I was like, 'Eh, I'm gonna do it.' If you're gonna be working then I'm gonna be working.

"So, I'm just gonna fly in and out it's gonna be awesome. I'm really looking forward to it," the Spiderwebs singer concluded.

MARRIAGE ISSUES

Blake and Gwen tied the knot in 2021 after six years together.

Rumors have recently swirled that the pair were having marriage problems.

In late December 2023, Gwen sparked concern when she wiped her social media profile of all photos of Blake.

"What happened to all the videos of you and Blake?" one fan asked in the comments of another post.

"I haven’t seen them either!" someone else agreed.

"Need to see some stuff with you and Blake together," chimed in a third.

Some followers speculated that the duo's supposed relationship issues could stem from Gwen not fitting into Blake's country lifestyle.
