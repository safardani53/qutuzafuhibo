Who is Willie D:

Willie D is a famous Rapper. He was born on November 1, 1966 and his birthplace is Houston, TX. Willie is also well known as, Born William Dennis, he is an American rapper and member of the Geto Boys who is best known for co-writing the legendary track “Mind Playing Tricks on Me.”

Willie is originated from United States. He was featured on the song “Down South Hustlaz” with Rick Ross.
