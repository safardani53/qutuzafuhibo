Julia Gilman Biography

Julia Gilman is a popular YouTube star is mainly known from her own YouTube account BeautyTakenIn. She is famous in the YouTube for posting the beauty and fashion videos. 

Young and talented, Julia Gilman was born on July 4, 1995, in California, the United States of America to American parents. She grew up in Los Angeles, California. She is also an American and is of white ethnicity.  She has a younger brother, Blake Gilam.

Julia has been active on YouTube since July 2011 after creating her own YouTube channel, . She began posting several beauty tips videos and fashion videos. As of 2018, her YouTube channel has over 2.63 million subscribers.

Besides her popularity in YouTube, she is also known for her appearance in 2013 BeautyCon event and in BeautyCon 2015 alongside LaurDIY and Mia Stammer. Often her success is also compared with highly-subscribed beauty vloggers on YouTube like Meredith Foster, Nicole Guerriero, Kathleen Lights and Bethany Mota.
