Who Is Wiz Khalifa Dating? Get To Know His Girlfriend Aimee Aguilar

After his split from Amber Rose on September 24, 2014, fans have since wondered, "who is Wiz Khalifa dating right now?"

The rapper has a huge reputation for dating famous women, including Canadian fashion model Winnie Harlow and media personality Khloe Kardashian. 

Harlow and Khalifa started dating in the summer of 2018, four years after his split from his ex-wife Rose, until they called off their relationship in October 2019. Soon after his breakup with the 27-year-old model, Khalifa was seen multiple times with a 'mystery woman.'

Who Is Wiz Khalifa’s New Girlfriend?

Khalifa's new girlfriend no longer remained a mystery when she posted a picture on her birthday, June 10, 2020, with Khalifa.

His new girlfriend is named Aimee Aguilar, and although we do not know much about her background, we certainly can say a few things about Khalifa's new girlfriend from her social media. 

Aguilar is an Instagram model who has made small cameos in shows like Impractical Jokers and Power. In addition, Aguilar has featured in several music videos like rapper Tyga's 'Taste,' and Quavo's 'HOW BOUT THAT?'

The social media personality is also an entrepreneur as she runs her clothing brand called 'Oasis Swim,' which sells swimwear at an affordable price of $25. 

More on Wiz Khalifa

Khalifa was born Cameron Jibril Thomaz on September 8, 1987, in Minot, North Dakota, to military parents. This meant that young Khalifa lived in different parts of the world, including Germany, the United Kingdom, and Japan.

After his parents divorced, he moved to Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, with his mother in 1996 and completed his education at Taylor Allderdice High School.

Khalifa was only 15 when he first tried his hands on music. His frequent visits to a studio called I.D. Labs earned him impressive reputations from the executives, as they allowed him to record his music for free.

He introduced himself by his name stage name, Wisdom Khalifa. Khalifa means success and is derived from Arabic. The rapper later shortened the 'Wisdom' in his name to Wiz.

Wiz Khalifa’s Rise to Fame

The rapper released his debut album, Show and Prove in 2006, and signed to Warner Bros. Records in 2007.

Khalifa's music earned him a reputation from the local audience, but he struggled to establish himself as a mainstream artist. All that changed with the release of his single in 2010 named 'Black and Yellow' that blew up overnight and even reached No.1 on Billboard Hot 100. 

The single was the lead track of his third studio album and remained at No.2 on Billboard 200 chart. After that, he made popular rap albums like O.N.I.F.C. (2012) and Blacc Hollywood (2014), which made him a household name in the world of hip hop and pop music. 

The musician was also appointed to write and record for the soundtrack of Fast and Furious 7. He debuted with the track 'Go Hard or Go Home' alongside rapper Iggy Azalea before earning his reputation for 'See You Again' featuring singer Charlie Puth.

The latter served as a tribute to late actor Paul Walker and peaked at No.1 on the Billboard 200.
