Hair perfume is now a thing and here are the best budget fragrances to try

FRAGRANCE for your hair – a totally unnecessary purchase or the ultimate act of indulgence?

While we certainly won’t claim that anyone NEEDS a fragrance specifically designed for luscious locks, if you are in need of a pick-me-up then look no further than our top five… all for under £25.

  • Percy & Reed Eau My Goodness Fragrance Spray, £22 from Feel Unique - Buy now

British brand Percy & Reed are leading the way in the hair perfume industry with an array of different fragrances.

Eau My Goodness (worth purchasing just for the pun) is delicate floral fragrance with dominant notes of roses and and freshly cut grass.

In other words, it’s effectively Spring captured in a spray.

Spritz on either dry or damp hair for extra shiny, incredibly-smelling locks.

  • Narciso Rodriguez For Her Hair Mist, £23.99 from Fragrance Direct - Buy now

Can’t bear to fork out £81 for the full-sized Narciso Rodriguez perfume?

Fear not, get the coveted For Her scent for a fraction of the original price with this hair fragrance.

Housed in a beautiful pink glass bottle that is pretty much made for Instagram, this musky amber fragrance leaves your hair smelling glorious without any oily residue.

  • Balmain Silk Hair Perfume, £24.25 from Look Fantastic - Buy now

A definite treat for your tresses, Balmain’s silk perfume smells of sweet and sultry Argan Oil that repairs damaged hair.

A fresh and Summery scent, this perfume leaves hair looked radiant and refreshed.

What a combo.

  • Toni & Guy Illuminating Hair Perfume, £8.25 from Superdrug - Buy now

Devoted Toni & Guy customers will be happy to hear that you can now get that delicious salon smell and shine in a bottle.

Throw this zesty perfume in your handbag for healthy-looking hair on the go.

  • Pink Sugar Hair Perfume, £10.30 from Amazon - Buy now

Almost certainly the sweetest scent of all time, Pink Sugar also now available in hair perfume form – for those who can’t quit handle their entire body smelling like candy floss.

Despite its sugary smell, this hair perfume is not at all sticky and works to protect dry hair and add shine.
