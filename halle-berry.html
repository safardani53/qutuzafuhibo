Halle Berry Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Bio:
Halle Berry (born Maria Halle Berry in Cleveland, Ohio on August 14, 1966) is an Emmy, Golden Globe, SAG and Academy Award-winning Actress. Her most notable films include Monster’s Ball, Dorothy Dandridge, Catwoman and Die Another Day. She was the first African American woman to ever win a Best Actress Academy Award. Before she became an actress, she was a fashion model and beauty pageant Queen. Today, Halle is one of the highest-paid actresses in Hollywood. She has been the face of Revlon and Versace. In November 2012, Berry came under fire when her ex boyfriend, Gabriel Aubry, and her current fiancé, Olivier Martinez, were involved in a graphic fight outside her house on Thanksgiving — with her 4-year-old daughter present.

Best Known For:
Halle is best known for being an award-winning actress.

Personal Life:
Halle was married to basketball player David Justice from 1993 to 1996. Halle’s second marriage was to Eric Benet, from 2001 to 2005. She started dating model Gabriel Aubry in November 2005, and with him had daughter Nahla Aubry on March 16, 2008. In April 2010, they separated and Halle began dating actor Olivier Martinez. They confirmed their engagement in March 2012 and married in July 2013. On Oct. 5, 2013, Halle gave birth to their first son, Maceo.
