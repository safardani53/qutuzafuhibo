The Range apologises after shoppers left 'shocked' at rude Christmas decoration

A SHOP has apologised for selling a Christmas tree doggy decoration — with his “bits” on show.

Bosses at The Range were “shocked” at the blunder — but the mutt has proved a hit.

It comes after customers pointed out the wooden sausage dog with gifts on his back is more than he first appears.

His privates are visible under his red and white festive jumper, if you look hard enough.

The Range said: “We’ve received a few messages about this product... Ooops!! We’re as shocked as all of you.”

One shopper said: “I need this little guy with his bits on show!”
