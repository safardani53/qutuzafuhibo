Moises Arias-Willow Smith Photo Prompts Investigation By Child Protective Services

Jada Pinkett-Smith may think there’s no cause to be concerned about the photo of her daughter Willow Smith in bed with Moises Arias, a 20-year-old actor and friend of the family, but the Los Angeles Department of Child and Family Services reportedly begs to differ.

A source tells Radar Online that Child Protective Services is investigating the Smith family in connection with the controversial photo that leaked online earlier this month.

“The investigation was formally opened last week and is being taken very seriously by the department,” says the insider.

“Will and Jada have been extremely cooperative with officials. Of course, they aren’t happy that their parenting skills are under scrutiny, but they understand.”

The source adds that both Willow and Arias will be interviewed by the authorities:

 

“Social workers will be talking separately with Willow, and they also want to talk to the young man in the picture with her as well. This won’t just be one or two visits with the family.”

“It will likely be an open investigation for at least a month.”

Jada Pinkett Smith claimed there was “nothing sexual” about the photo when she spoke to reporters at LAX shortly after the picture was revealed online.
