Who is Warren Sapp:

Warren Sapp is a famous Football Player. He was born on December 19, 1972 and his birthplace is Orlando, FL. Warren is also well known as, 7-time Pro Bowl defensive lineman who was honored as the NFL Defensive Player of the Year in 1999. He helped lead the Tampa Bay Buccaneers to victory in Super Bowl XXXVII.

Warren is originated from United States. He and Ray Lewis both starred at the University of Miami and went on to have successful NFL careers.
