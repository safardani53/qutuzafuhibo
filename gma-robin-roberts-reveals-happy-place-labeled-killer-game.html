GOOD Morning America host Robin Roberts has revealed her "happy place" in a recent Instagram post.

She went to Key West, Florida, for her birthday, after she was called a "killer" during an on-air pickleball game.

Robin shared a peaceful video of a body of water with a sunset behind it.

Two boats floated far away in the water and the waves moved rapidly.

The crashing waves could be heard in the video. as well as other background noise.

"One of the reasons why this is my happy place," the GMA host labeled the video.

She also followed with up with "#saturdayvibes."

The host celebrated another year of life in Florida, which she called her "happy place."

Robin shared another picture of the ocean the day before, where the sunset was not as intense, but just as beautiful.

"Grateful to begin another trip around the sun watching a beautiful sunrise. Wishing you and all you hold close to your heart a peaceful and #happythanksgiving #thankfulthursday," she labeled the post.

'BEAUTIFUL SMILE!'

For her birthday, Robin also shared an unrecognizable family throwback picture while in her "happy place."

She received dozens of birthday well-wishes from fans online and shared a photo from her niece Bianca on her Instagram Stories.

The throwback snap showed the pair beaming and it was captioned:  “Wayback Wednesday.”

The host replied: “My dear niece you still have the same beautiful smile.”

The morning show host revealed she had the “perfect ending” to a "wonderful" day.

She said that she made it to her “happy place” for a birthday meal and dessert.

Robin shared a picture showing slices of coconut chocolate chip bread pudding.

She said: “Thank you for all the well wishes and love. Returning to you tenfold.”

Fans wished Robin and her partner Amber Laign well ahead of the holidays in the comments section of the post.

The host revealed she was leaving early on Wednesday to head to Florida.

ROBIN'S WRATH

But before Robin left early to kick off a new year of life, she showed off her competitive side during an on-air game.

She enjoyed a game of pickleball with Becky Worley in the studio and it got rather tense.

Pickleball is a racket/paddle sport that can be played indoors or outdoors and sees players, hit a perforated hollow polymer ball over a 36-inch-high net using solid-faced paddles.

The two women were playing the ball game in a part of the studio that was set up like a bedroom.

"You know it's the rage, apparently it's one of the biggest searches on Google!" Becky shouted out as she and Robin hit the ball back and forth.

"Oh, she's beating me already! Woah woah, okay okay ready, she's good, she's a tennis player!" Becky added as Robin had a serious look of concentration on her face.

And when Robin batted the ball pretty hard and it went flying into the corner of the studio, Becky screamed: "She's a killer! She's a killer I'm telling you!"
