Mob Wives Season 4 Find Out Which Stars Quit Hollywood Life

‘Mob Wives’ Season 4 — Find Out Who Is Leaving The Show

You’re not going to believe it but Carla Facciolo, Karen Gravano and Ramona Rizzo have quit the show! HollywoodLife.com spoke to multiple insiders who confirmed that they’re leaving after a dispute with Renee Graziano and the show’s creator, Jennifer Graziano.

“The women wanted more money and it’s only fair,” the source tells us. “But Renee and her sister said there was none. It’s common for negotiations to take place at the beginning of each new season of filming but it didn’t go down as expected. The girls are shocked but ready for the next chapter in their lives. Carla has her wine, Karen has her dad coming home, they all have a lot going on.”

Also, the insider tells us that Drita D’avanzo was supposed to band with the other women during negotiations but she decided to return to the show for another season.

This is pretty shocking and we are surprised so many of the show favorites will not be coming back. We reached out to VH1 and we’re waiting to hear back.

Alicia DiMichelle Garafola — Meet The Newest ‘Mob Wife’

It’s clear the creators of Mob Wives are wanting to find a way to cause major onscreen drama this season and it sounds like ratings gold!

We told you that HollywoodLife.com has learned that Alicia, who lives in Southern New Jersey, has officially signed on and started filming. But this doesn’t sound like random casting. Longtime star of the show, Karen, is the daughter of ex-Gambino boss, Sammy ‘The Bull’ Gravano. The big issue here is that Sammy has admitted that he was the one who “arranged the hit” of Michele’s father-in-law, Edward Garafalo per the orders of Gabino mob boss John Gotti.

“Alicia has been filming and there’s a ton of drama since she is cast mates with a family enemy,” the source tells us. “It’s crazy but she’s loving it.”

It sounds like there’s a lot of drama going down! Sound off and tell us if you’re shocked these women are not coming back!
