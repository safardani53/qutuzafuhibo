Who is WWE star Alexa Bliss' husband, Ryan Cabrera?

MUSICIAN Ryan Cabrera and WWE star Alexa Bliss have been married since 2022.

By December 2023, Cabrera and his seven-time wrestling champion wife are expected to become first-time parents.

Who is Alexa Bliss' husband Ryan Cabrera?

Ryan Cabrera was born in Dallas, Texas, on July 18, 1982.

The 40-year-old attended the Jesuit College Preparatory School before enrolling at The University of Texas at Dallas.

While in college, Cabrera was a member of the popular band Rubix Groove.

Cabrera impressed a sound engineer after composing three Rubix Groove songs, earning him the opportunity to record his first solo project: Elm Stree (2001).

After signing with Atlantic Records, he released his hit single - On The Way Down - in 2004.

His debut album Take it All Away ranked at number eight on the Billboard 200 album chart and sold over 66,000 copies in the first week, certifying platinum.

Over the course of his music career, he has released four studio albums and one EP.

Outside of music, the singer became a household name when he appeared on popular MTV reality shows such as The Ashlee Simpson Show and The Hills.

Who is WWE star Alexa Bliss?

Alexa Bliss was born Alexis Kaufman in Columbus, Ohio, on August 9, 1991.

The 31-year-old grew up involved in sports, competing in track, kickboxing, softball, gymnastics, and cheerleading from the age of five.

Bliss reached Division 1 status in cheerleading at the University of Akron.

She signed with WWE in 2013 and quickly rose the ranks, making her main roster debut on SmackDown in 2016.

The 5-foot-1 athlete has made the RAW, SmackDown, and Tag Team championships a total of six times.

Being one of the top-ranking stars in the company, the "Goddess of WWE" has over five million Instagram followers.

She launched her own WWE-powered podcast, Uncool with Alexa Bliss, back in 2020.

In February 2021, WWE announced she and Charlotte Flair would guest star in Peacock's Punky Brewster reboot.

How long have Alexa Bliss and Ryan Cabrera been together?

Cabrera and Bliss dated for over a year before becoming engaged in November 2020.

The two were introduced through a mutual friend - Mike The Miz Mizanin - in October 2019 at the SmackDown on FOX premiere in California.

Soon after, they began dating and attended the 2020 New Year's Eve in St. Louis and that year's Grammy Awards.

On April 9, 2022, the pair officially walked down the aisle to say "I do."

According to PEOPLE, Cabrera and Bliss exchanged vows at the Kempa Villa in Palm Desert, California.

"It's very us. The whole thing's meant to bring out our personalities, and we are far from just normal," Cabrera told the outlet.

Both Cabrera and Bliss wore blush to their non-traditional, rockstar-themed wedding.

On May 30, 2023, Bliss took to Instagram to announce her pregnancy.

Who has Ryan Cabrera dated in the past?

Prior to his engagement to Alexa Bliss, Ryan Cabrera has been romantically linked to a multitude of celebrity women.
