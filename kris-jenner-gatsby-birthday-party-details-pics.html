All The Details &amp; Pics Hollywood Life

Kendall Jenner, 20, dressed up true to the theme as Gatsby’s love interest Daisy Buchanan, wearing a silver sequined mini-dress with matching headband and a blonde wig with vintage 1920s style waves. Sister Kylie, 18, went way more futuristic in a gorgeous silver gown with beads all over it. The glam look had a nude underlay that showed off her toned legs under the glittery patchwork. Her boyfriend Tyga, 25, kept is super classy in a white tuxedo jacket and black pants, and the couple made sure to pose elegantly for a photo in front of a Rolls Royce which the social media queen shared on her Instagram account.

Kourtney Kardashian 36, kept things simple in black pantsuit and white flowing blouse, claiming she was being Gatsby to Kendall’s Daisy! But she brought out the glam in her dramatic makeup, with dark red lips and smokey eyes. She also had her long dark locks completely slicked back in a tight bun behind her head. Sister Khloe, 31, was stunning in a white gown will silver linked overlays, and she even donned a matching cap of the silver print of her gown covering her blonde locks. So stylish!

Eight months pregnant Kim, 35, and husband Kanye West, 38, looked ever so elegant arriving at the bash. Kim stayed with the family’s glittery silver theme in a gorgeous gown with a plunging neckline while Yeezy looked so dapper in a black tux!

Over 300 guests are expected to attend the big bash, with celebrities such as Boy George and Chrissy Teigen seen arriving at the event in their best 1920’s Gatsby looks! The event is truly a throwback as vintage Rolls Royces were seen being delivered to the studios earlier in the day, so the party will be as authentic to its theme as possible!
