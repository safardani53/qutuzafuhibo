Eric Thomas Bolling is an American television personality, political commentator, author and conservative financial commentator. He has held multiple roles as a commentator on financial affairs for television, most notably for Fox News. He hosted Fox Business Channel's Cashin 'In and Fox News Channel's The Five before leaving that show to co-host Fox News Specialists in May 2017. As a staunch supporter of Donald Trump, Bolling has published two books: Wake Up America and The Swamp: Washington's heap of corruption and cronyism and how Trump can drain it. In September 2017, Bolling was ousted from Fox News, after the network conducted an independent investigation after an article on HuffPost reported that Bolling had submitted obscene photos and messages. request for three female colleagues. Bolling has denied the allegations.
