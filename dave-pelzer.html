Who is Dave Pelzer:

Dave Pelzer is a famous Memoirist. He was born on December 29, 1960 and his birthplace is Daly City, CA. Dave is also well known as, American author of memoirs and self-help books who is most famous for his autobiographical work, A Child Called It (1995). The book, which details Pelzer’s childhood of abuse, remained on the New York Times Bestseller List for years.

Dave is originated from United States. Olivia Colman’s character read his book, A Child Called It, in the British television series, Peep Show.
