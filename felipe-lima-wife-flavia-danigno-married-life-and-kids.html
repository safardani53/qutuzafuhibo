Felipe Lima Wife Flavia Danigno: Married Life And Kids

Felipe Lima’s wife, Flavia Danigno, works as a public defense lawyer in Brazil. Felipe and Flavia tied the knot in 2018 and share a baby girl. 

The Olympian and a celebrated athlete in his country, Brazil, Felipe became a first-time dad in 2022. After being married for four years, Felipe and Flavia welcomed their daughter Liz.

Flavia has been with the swimmer through thick and thin, and Felipe never misses an opportunity to show appreciation to his wife on his Instagram handle. 

The Brazilian swimmer has six World Championship medals under his belt and has contested in the Olympics. In the Tokyo Olympics heat, he almost broke the South American record and at the age of 36, set a personal record.

Felipe Lima Wife, Flavia Danigno

Felipe Lima and his wife, Flavia Danigna, have been married for five years. 

Like her husband, Flavia hails from the South American nation Brazil. On her Facebook, Flavia has shared several family pictures, including her husband and her parents. 

Flavia’s father, Marcos Alexandre Paranhos, graduated from the University of Brasilia with a mechanical engineering degree in 1981. He previously worked at Johson Controls as a regional manager.

He currently works at Almeida France Engineering as a Technical Director. Flavia followed in her father’s footsteps and graduated with a law degree from the University of Brasilia. 

She has been working as a Public Defense lawyer in the Federal District since 2014. Previously, on her Facebook, Flavia talked about the unfair judiciary system in her country.

She talked about how many innocent youths in her country are locked up for no reason. Flavia has also shared posts celebrating National Public Defender Day on her social media handle. 

Felipe Lima And Wife Flávia Danigno Relationship 

Felipe Lima and his wife, Flavia Danigno, went public with their relationship in 2016. 

But the couple might have been dating for a few years before making it official on Instagram. The swimmer became Instagram official with Flavia on the occasion of her birthday. 

He shared a sweet picture of them, and though he couldn’t be there to celebrate the day with her, Felipe sent his best wishes. A couple of months later, they took a Europe tour, celebrating their Christmas in Slovakia, and also had a mid-way stop in Hungary. 

Felipe And Flavia Engagement And Wedding 

The Brazilian swimmer decided to go down on one knee during their trip to Paris in 2017. He proposed to his long-time partner in the city of romance, in front of the Eiffel Tower.

Felipe shared the picture of him holding Flavia’s hand as he showed off the engagement ring with the Eiffel Tower in the background. Felipe captioned the post, “She said YES!!! Para sempre.”

The Olympian has made it a tradition to wish his wife on Valentine’s Day and her birthday. Hundred days before their wedding in 2018, Felipe shared a few pictures from their photoshoot. 

The couple tied the knot on May 18, 2018, in Federal District, Brazil. The ceremony was attended by several of their close friends and family members. 

Lima shared a series of pictures from their wedding ceremony, including a snap of Flavia walking down the aisle with her dad. 

The Couple Became Parents In 2022

On February 1, 2022, Felipe took to his Instagram to announce he was going to be a girl dad.

The Brazilian swimmer shared the picture of Flavia’s ultrasound and placed a pink emoji on it. He also shared a sweet snap of him kissing Flavia’s growing baby. 

On June 24, 2022, the couple welcomed their daughter, Liz, into the world. Flavia and Felipe shared several snaps of them holding their baby girl.

The couple celebrated their first Christmas as a family of three last year as Lima shared a gallery of cute images of his daughter and wife.
