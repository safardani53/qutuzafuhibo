Love &amp; Hip Hop Atlanta Season 6 Episode 15 Recap: Shifting Alliances

The drama in Jamaica continued to boil over for the stars when it became apparent there were some issues back home. 

When Love & Hip Hop Season 6 Episode 15 got underway, Jessica continued to show off just how happy she was about her engagement. Jessica then questioned Karlie about her feelings for Joc.

Karlie was adamant she just wanted him to remain loyal to her, as opposes to finding people to make her jealous with. They were interrupted by Mimi getting a phone call to say Stevie had Eva around Joseline. 

“He knew that I didn’t want that woman around my daughter and he waited until I was in Jamaica to do it,” she barked. The other women could only look in in disgust at his blatant act of desperation. 

When Mimi returned home, she wasted no time in visiting her ex-boyfriend to lay down the law. 

“I thought I told you to keep Joseline away from Eva," Mimi began.

"Then I get a phone call from my baby telling me that she had a great time with her baby sister.”

"Maybe Vonna took her over there, and Joseline was there for a minute,” Stevie replied.

Mimi says, “I don’t care if it was for one second. I am now going to have to go take out a protective order to try and keep this woman away from my child because you won’t listen!”

If Stevie was wanting to stop seeing Joseline as much, she had other plans. They met up, and she wasted no time in asking him to join her. 

"I want you to go back to being my manager. I made great music with you,” Joseline says.

Stevie replies, “I have new talent with them, and I have contracts and obligations.”

“Tell her that she is done working with you, and you’ll get your wife and your artist back because I know she isn’t as talented as the Puerto Rican Princess,” Joseline fires back.

Stevie tells her “Before we discuss anything you need to apologize to my daughters. They were really upset by the stuff you put on social media.”

Shockingly, Joseline agrees to apologize. Maybe she does want to be back in Stevie’s good books. 

Meanwhile, Rod was furious after a confrontation with Logan about Jasmine. He then returned to Jasmine to find out the truth. 

“When was the last time you slept with Logan? he asks.”

Jasmine says “2013.” Rod says “Not in 2015 at all?”

Jasmine claimed she did not sleep with him in 2015, so Rod offers up a DNA test for her to prove it. Jasmine fumed, but she decided to do it. 

When Stevie told his daughters about Joseline, they were repulsed. When she showed up, and Joseline apologized, they stormed out. 

They were done with Stevie and with her. 

Yikes. 

What do you think it will take for them to make things right with one another?
