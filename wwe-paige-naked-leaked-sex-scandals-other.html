After WWE Diva Paige's naked pictures were leaked, here are seven other sex tape scandals which rock

WWE has been rocked by a sex tape scandal involving British superstar Paige these past few days.

But its not the first time that company’s top stars have had explicit videos and photos released or leaked on the internet.

We take a look back at seven of the most notorious incidents, after Paige's sex tapes and pictures were leaked onto the internet.

One Night In Chyna

Joanie 'Chyna' Laurer left WWE in 2001, after claiming former boyfriend Triple H has started a secret affair with Stephanie McMahon.

Three years later she filmed a homemade sex tape with then-lover Sean 'X-Pac' Waltman, which was then sold to Red Light District Video and sold over 100,000 copies.

They followed up with a sequel Another Night in Chyna five years later.

Way-Hey Mickie

Mickie James has recently returned to WWE.

But before her original run with the company – which began in 2005 – she posed for several explicit photo shoots, which have been a hot topic among fans online for years.

She was also the centre of a locker room scandal, when former fiancée and Spirit Squad member Kenny Dykstra revealed she’d had an affair with John Cena in 2007.

The Hulkster Gets Some Bang For His Bucks

The most famous (and expensive) wrestling sex tape of them.

Hulk Hogan was secretly filmed with the wife of his friend and DJ “Bubba The Love Sponge” Clem.

The video appeared on gossip website Gawker in 2012.

The Hulkster sued for invasion of privacy and was awarded £92million, though settled for £28million after Gawker went bankrupt.

It cost Hogan in other ways though – the tape recorded several racial slurs, which caused WWE to fire the disgraced star in 2015.

Queen of the Ring

Chyna returned to porn in 2011, this time with professional production studio Vivid Entertainment.

She made several films – including a superhero parody playing the She-Hulk – but most controversial was Queen of the Ring, performing in a scene alongside porn versions of Vince and Stephanie McMahon. WWE claimed that Chyna’s porn career would mean she’d never enter the Hall of Fame.

The tragic star died of an overdose in 2016.

Seth Scandal

Nude photos of NXT Diva Zhara Schreiber and Seth Rollins appeared across the internet in February 2016.

Rollins’ (now ex) fiancé Leighla Schultz posted them across Instagram and Twitter and she discovered an alleged affair between Rollins and Schreiber.

Months before the biggest push of his career, Rollins was lucky to escape unscathed – and tweeted an apology to his fans for the photo leak.

Sunny Days

WWE’s original sex bomb Tamara 'Sunny' Sytch has been no stranger to scandal.

She’s had numerous arrests, substance abuse problems, and wrote a tell-all book revealing raunchy details about her affair with former WWE Champion Shawn Michaels.

For several years now she has offered one-to-one sessions with fans across Skype – and last year recorded a hardcore adult film for Vivid called Sunny Side Up.

Paige Turner

British star and former Divas Champion Paige has been the victim of a major hack this past weekend, with private videos and images leaked online.

The clips also feature former referee and wrestler Brad Maddox – plus a threesome including The New Day member Xavier Woods.

Most scandalous is a picture in which a sex act has been performed on a WWE championship belt.

With two drugs violations already, fans are wondering if Paige’s WWE career will survive this latest scandal.
