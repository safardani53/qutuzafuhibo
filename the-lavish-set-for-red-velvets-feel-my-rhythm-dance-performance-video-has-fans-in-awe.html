The Lavish Set For Red Velvets Feel My Rhythm Dance Performance Video Has Fans In Awe

With the release of their newest mini-album, The Reve Festival Feel My Rhythm, the world-famous K-pop girl group Red Velvet made their long-awaited homecoming. For its fascinating and unusual ballerina idea, the group’s CD has become the buzz of the town.

The group’s new dance performance video for the title tune, Feel My Rhythm, has gone viral due to its wonderful and colorful cinematic backdrop. In the beautifully crafted set, the girls spun and displayed their impressive dancing movements.

The stunning Ballroom Set Was Created By Red Velvet for the Feel My Rhythm dance performance video.

On March 23, 2022, the K-pop girl group released a dance performance video for the title tune Feel My Rhythm. The girls are seen in different ballerina stances at the start of the video, humming to the tune of Bach’s Air On G String with polished string music that gradually blends with an explosive trap rhythm.

Each member of the ensemble was dressed in glistening black, white, and gold outfits with crystal decorations embroidered into their voluminous gowns for the dance performance video. The females completed their ensembles with stylish glittering trinkets on their heads and knee-length black leather boots.

To go with their outfits, each member was given a unique and gorgeous hairdo. The females rocked a variety of styles, from beach-wave curls to carefully fastened braids and buns. The K-pop girl group’s dance performance took place in a large Victorian-style ballroom, which wonderfully complimented the album’s mystical ballerina idea. Red drapes and enormous candle stands adorned the high-ceilinged ballroom.

There were also several gold chandeliers hanging from the ceilings, which were vividly lighted when the troupe did their energetic dancing. A spotlight shines brightly on each group member at the end of the performance video, revealing their wonderful visuals and highlighting their breathtaking outfits.

Red Velvet’s dance performance video gets a ReVeluvs reaction

The stunning stage design, costumes, accessories, and makeup in Red Velvet’s dance performance video have left viewers in amazement. Fans flocked to social media to express their delight, claiming that the performance set transformed the group members into royal princesses.

The CD includes six tracks that highlight each group member’s individual strengths and abilities. Both the ReVe and the Oregel editions of the physical LPs feature distinct Red Velvet-themed goods.
