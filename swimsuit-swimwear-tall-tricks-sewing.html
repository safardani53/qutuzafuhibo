I'm an extremely tall girl at 6ft - my one-piece swimsuits always give me a wedgie so I turned it in

A CRAFTY TikToker has shared how she transformed her one-piece swimsuit into a two-piece to fit her tall girl style.

Even though she towered over others, the handy influencer with impressive height didn't want that to cause her to suffer in her swimwear.

Instead, she used a simple skill to refashion her swimsuit into a two-piece tankini - and she didn't have to skimp on style.

Lorelei (@lorelei.pryor) gave followers a look at the blue swimsuit that featured pink and white flowers, as well as olive green leaves, as she aired her grievances with the crotch-grabbing one-piece.

"If you are an extremely tall girl, like me, and you have a one-piece bathing suit, it probably doesn't fit the length of your torso, and every time you wear it, it gives you wedgie issues," she explained.

She then took to getting started on the swimsuit makeover with a pair of scissors.

"Today, I'm turning this one-piece into a tankini," she explained.

She also shared a piece of advice before embarking on the soon-to-be two-piece: "Decide the length of the top and bottom when cutting."

Her tall-girl takeover continued as she cut along the edge with her sewing scissors while making sure that she left enough room for the bottoms.

She also advised followers to "sew lining to top fabric," as she could be seen holding the thread out as she needled away.

Even without all of her equipment, she was still able to get her handiwork on to get a height-appropriate suit.

"Because I'm working at summer camp, I didn't have my sewing machine, so I'm hand sewing everything," she explained, with her wooden bunk in the background.

A fellow camper - or counselor - even appeared in the background as she gave a greeting while she continued to creatively work around her non-equipment constraints.

"I'm hand-sewing at the top," Lorelei explained, as she altered the swimsuit to flatter her longer legs.

She decided to get to the bottom of the bottoms another day, as she didn't have any of the needed elastic to complete the project.

The TikToker then turned around to give viewers a close-up of her transformed - and tall girl-approved look as she commented: "I'm really happy with it."

She also wore brown shorts as she explained how her last video - where she wore nothing but a swimsuit - got taken down.

Many of her over 58,000 followers were also fans of the new suit.

"Omg I needed this," commented one viewer.
