Champion Bumper trends

Ignore beaten favourites last time out.

Stick with horses that have never been out of the first two.

Five and six-year-olds are the ages to focus on.

Willie Mullins has a great record but not with his most fancied runner.

Short-priced favourites have a poor record.

When is the Champion Bumper?

The race takes place at 5.30pm on Wednesday, March 16.

ITV will be showing coverage of the Festival and every race will be broadcast live on Racing TV.
