What plastic surgery has Madonna had?

MADONNA has constantly revamped her look over the years.

The superstar looks better than ever, and some fans have speculated if the singer has had work done.

What plastic surgery has Madonna had?

In February 2023, Madonna finally admitted to getting plastic surgery in a social media post.

The singer posted a picture of her on Twitter and made a reference to the fact many thought she "didn't look like herself" at the Grammy Awards.

She wrote to her 2.8 million followers: “Look how cute I am now that swelling from surgery has gone down. Lol.”

Madonna didn’t reveal any further details on the nature of the procedure she had done.

How much has Madonna spent on plastic surgery?

As Madonna has never confirmed the work she has had done, any price tag is unknown.

However, cosmetic procedures don't come cheap.

They can range from hundreds, to thousands and thousands of dollars - especially if you go to top plastic surgeons and dermatologists.
