A DIETICIAN has revealed the 13 items they purchase each week at Trader Joe's while keeping their bill under $50.

While adjusting to new prices at grocery stores across the country, the diet expert shared the inexpensive items they swear by at Trader Joe's.

The dietician, speaking to Insider, said that she's also a bride-to-be who likes shopping on a budget without sacrificing great food.

She began her list of 13 items with a versatile protein.

Lentils - 1lb for $3.29

Lauren Armstrong writes that lentils are a healthy, savory option for any meal, and Trader Joe's offers them in soup form, which you can serve hot or cold.

For both vegetarians and meat eaters, these lentils are a great option for protein consumption.
