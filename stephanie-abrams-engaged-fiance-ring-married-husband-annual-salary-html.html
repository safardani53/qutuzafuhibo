Stephanie Abrams Status After Engaged With Fiance; Married Or Not?

American television meteorologist Stephanie Abrams was always successful in achieving noticeable recognition in her professional career, and besides that, she dropped a hint about planning on making a prosperous family life with her fiance.

Stephanie Abrams is an American television meteorologist, mostly recognized as the on-camera meteorologist of The Weather Channel. She has been with the network since 2003.

Stephanie is a member of the American Meteorological Society (AMS) and the National Weather Association (NWA) and from the AMS, she holds the seal of approval.

Stephanie Abrams’ Bio: Education & Career as Meteorologist

Stephanie, who was born on 27 October 1978 in Wellington, Florida, graduated with a Bachelor of Science in geology, minoring in mathematics, from the University of Florida in Gainesville. She also received a second B.S degree in meteorology from Florida State University in Tallahassee.

Stephanie’s first professional job was as a morning meteorologist at WXTK, the ABC affiliate in Tallahassee, Florida, where she worked from 2002 to 2003.

Later on July 2003, she joined The Weather Channel as a reporter. She worked as a reporter and fill-in on the channel till 2005. She received her first show, Weekend View, as a co-host in 2005. Finally, in 2006, she became on-camera meteorologist as co-host of Abrams and Bettes: Beyond the Forecast and then went on to Weather Center, also with co-host Mike Bettes.

Since then she has worked in diverse departments of the network and also received some honorable recognition. She has even done live reporting for the 2010 Winter Olympics in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada.

Presently, Stephanie, who stands at the height of 1.73 meters, works at the station The Weather Channel which she joined in 2003 and also co-hosts AMHQ. As per the Payscale, she receives an average annual salary of $82,000 from The Weather Channel.

Stephanie Reports With Elmo On Hurricane Florence

On 11 September 2018, Stephanie talked about the basic notions of a hurricane, following the Hurricane Florence that is bearing down on the east coast of the United States and she was joined by the famous muppet character, Elmo.

As families were bracing for the category four hurricane, the Weather Channel and Sesame Workshop joined hands to help parents talk to their children on how to stay prepared.

Elmo joined Stephanie to talk about what hurricanes are and who to turn to for help in emergencies. According to the Weather Channel, Florence was projected to make landfall within northern South Carolina and North Carolina's Outer Banks on 13 September 2018.
