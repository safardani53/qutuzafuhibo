The Golden Wedding: Where to watch Gerry Turner and Theresa Nist tie the knot

GERRY Turner and Theresa Gist have been preparing for their nuptials following their engagement.

Now, fans want to know more about the couple's wedding and how to tune in to the highly-anticipated TV ceremony.

Where can I watch The Golden Wedding?

The Golden Wedding will premiere live on ABC on Thursday, January 4, 2024, at 8 p.m. EST.

Fans can also watch the television special — which will be hosted by Jesse Palmer — via the ABC app and via the network's website.

It will also be available for streaming on Hulu the following day.

ABC's description of the program states, in part: "After more than 20 years of fostering love on 'The Bachelor,' 'The Bachelorette' and 'Bachelor in Paradise,' 'The Golden Bachelor' showcases a whole new kind of love story – one for the golden years.

"On this all-new unscripted series, one hopeless romantic is given a second chance at love in the search for a partner with whom to share the sunset years of life.

"After lifetimes of love and loss and a whirlwind journey on 'The Golden Bachelor,' Gerry Turner and Theresa Nist will walk down the aisle to begin their next chapter, happily ever after."

When did Gerry Turner and Theresa Nist get engaged?

Gerry Turner and Theresa Nist got engaged during the season 1 finale of The Golden Bachelor, which premiered on November 30, 2023.

According to Cosmopolitan, Turner "presumably proposed" during the week of August 30, 2023.
