Seraphina Affleck Hollywood Life

Bio:
Seraphina Affleck (born Seraphina Rose Elizabeth Affleck in Los Angeles, CA on January 6, 2009) is the middle child of actors Ben Affleck and Jennifer Garner. She has one older sister, sister Violet Affleck, and one younger brother Samuel Garner Affleck.

Best Known For:
Being an exact replica of her older sister Violet, but with brunette hair.

Personal Life:
Seraphina was only 6-years-old when her parents announced their divorce on June 30, 2015.
