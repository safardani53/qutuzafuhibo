Today Show Halloween Costumes 2015: Hosts Dress Up Peanuts Characters

Good grief! The Today show plaza turned into a comic strip on Friday, Oct. 30, when the co-hosts showed off their matching Halloween costumes. This year’s theme: Peanuts! 

.@WillieGeist as Schroeder tickling the ivories! #HalloweenTODAY pic.twitter.com/cIlEjWjjwn

— TODAY (@TODAYshow) October 30, 2015

The big reveal started off with Willie Geist playing the piano as Schroeder before moving to a classroom where Peppermint Patty (Tamron Hall) and Marcie (Natalie Morales) were arguing. 

Linus or Gollum? (We love you @CarsonDaly!) #HalloweenTODAY pic.twitter.com/aqGn8451Df

— TODAY (@TODAYshow) October 30, 2015

.@NMoralesNBC and @tamronhall are…Marcie & Peppermint Pattie! #HalloweenTODAY pic.twitter.com/byNYrKGARa

— TODAY (@TODAYshow) October 30, 2015

Then Linus (Carson Daly sporting a bizarre headpiece) and Sally (Savannah Guthrie) hit the dance floor. But the best reveal happened when Al Roker came out as Charlie Brown and Matt Lauer as his often disagreeable pal, Lucy. 

.@AlRoker is…Charlie Brown! #HalloweenTODAY pic.twitter.com/IDx9qzssmz

— TODAY (@TODAYshow) October 30, 2015

.@MLauer is… Lucy! #HalloweenTODAY pic.twitter.com/LKRi9QAusE

— TODAY (@TODAYshow) October 30, 2015

Wine-loving besties Hoda Kotb and Kathie Lee Gifford were completely unrecognizable as Snoopy the dog and his bird friend Woodstock, and Today show alum Meredith Vieira returned to play a comical Pigpen. 

.@HodaKotb & @KathieLGifford are…Snoopy & Woodstock! #HalloweenTODAY pic.twitter.com/Sbb8BjUUBo

— TODAY (@TODAYshow) October 30, 2015

The elaborate looks followed last year’s Saturday Night Live-themed costumes, and certainly didn’t disappoint.
