A Place in the Sun's Laura Hamilton dazzles fans in 'red hot' dress at live show

A PLACE In The Sun host Laura Hamilton wowed fans in her red tiered summer dress at the property show's exhibition in London.

The Channel 4 host never fails to impress her loyal legion of followers with her stunning snaps on social media.

Laura, 48 did not disappoint this time as she dazzled fans at the live A Place in the Sun live show.

The A Place in the Sun host took to Instagram to post a reel from the exhibition and give her 183K followers an insight into the live event.

She showcased a stunning red maxi dress perfect for the summer weather.

The statement pieced flaunted a tiered design, a bardot off the shoulder neckline and paisley print.
