Who is TikTok star Kristen Hanby?

KRISTEN Hanby is known for his pranks all over the world.

He has a huge following on YouTube and TikTok, but how did he find fame?

Who is Kristen Hanby?

Kristen Hanby is a content creator, singer and prankster.

He was born on March 8, 1993, in St Helier, Jersey, in the Channel Islands.

Hanby has now moved to in Los Angles, California.

Since 2019 he is in relationship with social media influencer Jasmine Brownsword.

What does Kristen Hanby do on TikTok and YouTube?

Hanby started his career by posting pranks and jokes he performed on his family and friends on YouTube.

He opened his YouTube channel in September 2011, however he didn't upload his first video until 2013.

His first video went viral overnight.

Hanby includes up to five pranks in one video, which makes him different than other pranksters.

After going viral he decided to create more videos and he started pranking his family, friends and strangers.

Sometimes he gets pranked by his family or friends.

As of June 2023, he has 3.36 million subscribers on YouTube.

Hanby started to share his pranks on TikTok in 2019 - and hasn't stopped since.

Next to TikTok and YouTube he is also on Instagram, where he shares his lifestyle with his followers.

As of June 2023 he has 7.1 million followers on Instagram and 13.5 followers on TikTok.

What is Kristen Hanby net worth?

Hanby's net worth is $8million (£6.4million).

Due to his popularity he has sponsorships with many commercial brands.

Hanby had a sponsorship with footballer Jesse Lingard and his merch promotion.

He said during an interview with Square Mile: "Lingard's PA asked me to do a bit of merch promotion, so they sent me some out.

"And then I went to his clothing launch in Manchester.

"I met Jesse and [Marcus] Rashford, and more.

"But Jesse's the one who was the friendliest to me.

"I'm a big Man U fan, so it's surreal being invited to all their games and meeting all the players."

What did Kristen Hanby do before becoming an influencer?

Before becoming an influencer Hanby was working full-time on a building site in Jersey.

He grew up with his mother Giselle, his two sisters Natalie and Bryony, and his brother Jonathan.
