Terrell Owens Monthly Child Support $44K, Tells Judge He's Broke

Six-time NFL Pro Bowler Terrell Owens has found himself in the middle of yet another child support dispute. The wide receiver is currently a free agent and claims to have no money.

In one of his court cases, T.O. filed legal documents claiming that he doesn’t have enough money after expenses to cover attorney fees for the baby mama.

The guy actually has a lucrative gig tweeting for big brands. In recently filed docs, he claims he brings in between $2,800 and $6,800 per endorsement tweet for Cutters, Old Navy and Fantapper.

Owens also pulling in periodic residuals for his appearances on Punk’d, Any Given Sunday and Necessary Roughness and declared to the court that he raked in $10K for promoting Yahoo Fantasy Football and another $20K for promoting a Super Bowl Party.

So why can’t he afford to pay the attorney fees for a baby mama? You do the math … his average monthly expenses are listed at $82,000 plus an additional $44,602 for child support.

Earlier this month a California judge issued a bench warrant against Owens after he failed to show at a different child support hearing. He promptly made good on his overdue child support and the warrant was dropped.

Terrell has no wives or ex-wives to his credit but he is baby daddy to sons Terique and Atlin, as well as daughters Kylee and Dasha.
