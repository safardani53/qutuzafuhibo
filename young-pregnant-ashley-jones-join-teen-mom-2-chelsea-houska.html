Young + Pregnant's Ashley Jones will join Teen Mom 2 after Chelsea Houska quits

MTV’s Ashley Jones is reportedly the newest cast member on Teen Mom 2 after first being introduced on Young + Pregnant in 2018. 

Two years later, Ashley, 23, will transition from Teen Mom: Young + Pregnant to the spinoff with Kailyn Lowry, Leah Messer, Briana DeJesus and her former costar, Jade Cline.

The reality star made her television debut in 2018 when the show documented the birth of her first daughter, Holly. 

In addition to welcoming her daughter - who is not three - Ashley showed the struggles of her on-again-off-again relationship with baby daddy, Bariki Smith. 

Now, the MTV star will be moving on to Teen Mom 2, production sources told People. 

Ashley will be the second Young + Pregnant star to make the transition as nemesis Jade, 23, also went from reality show to reality show. 

In December 2019, the pair got into a heated confrontation as their tense feud escalated during the MTV reunion 

Ashley claimed she previously got an offer to join the series, and shouted: ”You accuse me of lying about being on Teen Mom 2!

“I didn't lie about it. I was actually asked if I wanted to go. I said I would rather stay with YP.

"I was more than supportive of your move to Teen Mom 2 until you said what you said. I felt shaded by that because first of all, I don't have to lie.”

Jade fired back: ”I didn't say s**t to you!"

“No, you didn't because you don't have the balls," her frenemy screamed. 

Ashley will be replacing fan-favorite Chelsea Houska, 29, - who announced her departure from the MTV series after 10 seasons earlier this month. 

The mom-of-three shared a photo with her husband Cole, their daughter Layne, 2, son Watson, 3, and her daughter Aubree, 11, with ex-boyfriend Adam Lind.

She captioned the post: “MTV’s Teen Mom 2 has been a big part of my life for almost 11 years. After much thought and discussion with my family and friends, Cole and I have decided that this season will be our last. 

“We are forever thankful to MTV and our crew, who are like family to us. We’re parting on the best of terms and will stay in touch long after this. 

“We’re proud to have been able to share our story and are so grateful to the fans who have followed our journey from the beginning.  

She added that the “next chapter” of their life will be to “focus on developing our brand and taking things to the next level with new endeavors and expanding family businesses.”

The red-haired star ended the statement with: "Please tune into our last moments on the show and continue to follow along our journey on social media. 

“We are so excited for this next phase of our lives and hope you all will be a part of it in some way!”
