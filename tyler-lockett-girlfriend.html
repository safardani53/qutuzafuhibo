A Short Bio on Tyler Lockett

The Seattle Seahawks wide receiver Tyler Lockett was born on September 28, 1992, in Tulsa, Oklahoma, United States.

Born to Nicole and Kevin Lockett, Tyler spent his childhood in Tulsa and attended Barnard Elementary School for his primary schooling.

His Father, Kevin, was a former football player for Kansas State, which inspired young Tyler to engage in extracurricular activities from an early age. Apart from football, he was good at basketball and track and field.

Despite his short height, he led his school basketball team and reached the state championship, too. However, in the long run, he chose football and later played college football for the Kansas State Wildcats.

Lockett played all four seasons for the Wildcats until 2014, where he won several honors and awards. He has already received the Kansas State Wildcats Ring of Honor for his exceptional game during his college career.

Tyler has been playing in the NFL for the Seahawks since 2015. The club selected him as the 69th overall pick in the third round of the 2015 NFL draft.
