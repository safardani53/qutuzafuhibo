How Much Does Melissa Earn From Her Work At 6ABC? Find Out Below

Reporting and anchoring have always been Melissa Magee’s passion ever since she was a little girl. Her constant perseverance has led her to achieve what she has always wanted. She first began her career at a CBS/Fox affiliate in California and was later promoted to noon anchor at KBAK-TV. From then onwards she moved to New York where she started her job at WNYW as a weather reporter for the networks midday newscast. In 2009, she made another leap which saw her land a job at 6abc action news team where she works as a weekend meteorologist.

Magee has had quite a career behind her. So, when it comes to paychecks and how much she earns, it would come as no surprise to see a paycheck with numbers well above average. The exact details of her salary have not yet been disclosed but as per reports of paysa, the average salary of a 6abc employee is $78,593. No doubt Melissa earns more than the average employee with the career that she has had.
