Im a fashion pro - how to turn ANY bra into a push-up using a piece of string

Getting a lifted look without the bra can seem impossible.

Thankfully, one TikTok user has found a unique and affordable hack to achieve the push-up effect.

TikTok account @AmericanThreads is the online counterpart to a boutique shop.

The viral video, which garnered almost 200,000 likes, shared this simple bra hack.

The model started the TikTok by grabbing a string and looping it into her single bra strap.

She then weaved the string under her other strap.

The model pulled both ends of the string, which brought her bra straps and breasts together.

She tightened the loop and brought it over her head, tying a knot at the base of her neck.

She then tucked the knot into her top, hiding the string.

After doing so, the string was hardly visible.
