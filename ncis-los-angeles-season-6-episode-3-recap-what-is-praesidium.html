NCIS Los Angeles Season 6 Episode 3 Recap: A Marked Woman

Having previously funneled government money to a terrorist to rescue one of her own, Hetty Lange made a bold move and would probably do so again.

But there are consequences.

The events of NCIS: Los Angeles Season 6 Episode 3 continued to show us fallout caused by Hetty, whose chickens are coming home to roost in a big way.

Watch NCIS: Los Angeles Season 6 Episode 3 Online

She’s stuck in D.C. getting grilled on this. The DOJ wants to prove fault in actions. The department has sent in someone to question her team, as well.

Chief Investigator Ava Wallace wants the dirt and she wants it now, but more alarmingly, someone broke into one of Hetty’s homes. Yes, she’s got a bunch.

She moves around so as to leave no trace, but one of the addresses found its way onto the black market, spurring the NCIS team into action immediately.

Perhaps the plot to target her is both sinister and bicoastal, however. Hetty collapses in her meeting with the DOJ, leaving her health in peril … or is it?

 

Wallace kept interfering with the case, which led to an intense confrontation with Sam, who made it clear where he stands and what he thinks of her mission.

She ups the ante, though, and tries to arrest Callen. Yes, arrest him. Although just as defiantly, he chose to escape rather than give in to this witch hunt.

With Kensi left to answer Wallace’s questions – and she is forced to answer a few of them – the rest of the team sets up a plan to capture Hetty’s would-be captors.

Meanwhile, it turns out her fainting spell was a ruse to escape the tribunal. With her personal security guard dead, she had to know what was going on.

Then the big twist revealed: Leon Vance was behind this investigation. There’s a leak within NCIS and he knew he had to get Hetty out of L.A. immediately.

It’s still unclear who the leak is, and with her in D.C., the situation back home escalates as the team barely survives its own sting designed to catch the killers.

Moreover, Matthias is back in the country. With Hetty’s address(es) now available, it’s open season and all her old foes are coming out of the woodwork.
