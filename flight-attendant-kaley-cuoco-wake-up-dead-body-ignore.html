The Flight Attendant fans left 'screaming in shock' as Kaley Cuoco wakes up next to dead body and co

THE Flight Attendant viewers were left completely baffled by Kaley Cuoco as her character woke up to a man dead in her bed - then proceeded to ignore his body and clean the hotel room instead.

Viewers of the HBO Max mini series were divided between finding Cassie Bowden's conduct hilarious - while others thought it plain scary.

The plot centres on Kaley's air stewardess character waking up in a hotel room in Dubai with as dead man - who has his throat ungraciously slit - in the blood-strewn sheets next to her.

She is said to have no memory of what actually happened on the night.

Yet some fans couldn't believe she would go about her life as normal after the discovery, with one taking to Twitter to write: "Lmfao I’m screaming! The fact that she acting like she ain’t know she woke up to this man dead!"

Another added: "You take a guy back to your hotel room, have fun, wake up and the man is dead. Throat slit. Blood all over. Your next move is to…….clean up the hotel room????"

A third then mused: "I don't know how I would have reacted to a dead body, but I want to believe I would have handled it better than Cassie."

One then posted: "Wait, so she literally just gone leave a dead body in the hotel and go to work."

Another clearly gripped viewer then surmised: "Any show that begins with someone waking up from a drunken night laying next to a dead body and they don't know what happens. I'm in."

Meanwhile, Kaley, 34, previously told how she had to take down a few notes from her on-screen lover Michiel Huisman to make her first on screen sex scene look more believable.

Kaley is most well known for her role as Penny on the popular CBS sitcom The Big Bang Theory - which rarely got to see her get down on dirty on screen like in The Flight Attendant.

The star certainly had to turn the temperature up a notch whilst filming a sex scene with Michiel - who plays the stewardess' love interest Alex Sokolov - in a plane toilet.

When the time came for the big scene and she wasn't really giving it her all, The Big Bang Theory star told: "Michiel finally goes, 'It looks like you’re hovering over a public toilet. What are you doing?'

"I’m like, 'I don’t know what I’m doing'. So he had to awkwardly teach me how to have fake sex."
