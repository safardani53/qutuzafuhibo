LOVE Island star Rebecca Gormley strips off to her undies — before getting an unwelcome break.

The model, 22, is recovering after fracturing her arm in a fall.

She said: “I’m just so angry inside and hate not being able to do things for myself.

"I hate it so much but just hope I can have a speedy recovery and that it heals itself quicker than expected because it’s awful, man.”

She recently said in a Q&A she is keen to have a baby with boyfriend Biggs Chris, 28, who she met on last year’s Winter Love Island.

The pair were at the centre of rumours she was pregnant last October after Biggs shared a loved-up post on Instagram.

Sharing the adorable photo of him snuggled up to his Rebecca, Biggs captioned the snap: “Yo that’s baby mama uno”

In the photo Rebecca has her eyes closed as she rests her head lovingly on Biggs’ shoulder and he gazes into the camera.
