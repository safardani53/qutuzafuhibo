Victoria's Secret star Adriana Lima hits back at surgery claims saying she's a 'tired mum of five'

VICTORIA'S Secret model Adriana Lima has lashed out at speculation she's had surgery, claiming she's a "tired mum of five."

The catwalk queen - who once left boxing champ Tyson Fury starstruck - clapped back after being photographed on the red carpet.

Adriana, 42, donned a black mini-dress and pointed heels for the swanky Los Angeles event for the latest Hunger Games movie, The Hunger Games: The Ballad Of Songbirds & Snakes.

Yet talk turned from her frock to her appearance, with some fans quizzing "what has she done to her face?"

Victoria's Secret favourite Adriana faced the same flack earlier this week after uploading a snap showing her in a shimmering silver dress.

One Instagram troll was quick to comment: "What did you do to your gorgeous face? It looks different?"

Another speculated: "She did something to her face, Botox, Fillers. She’s not the same."

A third then put: "Our beauty was god given! I pray these were just fillers and they will dissolve…

"You’re still the most beautiful but it breaks my heart."

Not willing to let the trolling lie, Adriana hit back on her page.

She posted a completely make-up free snap of her face and wrote: "The face of a tired mom of one teenage girl, two preteens, a one-year-old learning to walk, and three dogs.

"Thanks for your concern."

The lingerie model previously told how she wants people to "look beyond" her appearance.

The supermodel has made millions from her incredible figure, but after embarking on a new spiritual lifestyle, she wants people to appreciate her mind, rather than her looks.

She posted a photo of The Art Of Living founder and guru Sri Sri Ravi Shanka speaking onstage to her Instagram page.

Alongside, the Brazilian wrote: "My thoughts after his teaching 'I want people to look at me beyond my face/body as Sri Sri says a mind has no face and body, and with that said you become closer to reality and the truth.'"

Meanwhile, Adriana welcomed her fifth child, her son Cyan Lima Lemmer into the world last summer.

She announced this birth on September 5, 2022, via her Instagram.

In the Instagram post, along with a photo of her son's eye, the caption read: "Cyan is the color between green and blue on the visible spectrum of light.

"Cyan is the color of the waters of Bora Bora and Maldives, places in our family’s bucket list.

"Cyan now is our favorite color… the color of our baby boy’s eyes.
