6 Best Cross Stitch Kits For Adults 2020 | The Sun UK

CROSS STITCH is one of those wonderful home crafting activities that you can pick up at any point - and there are plenty of beginners' kits to snap up online.

The best cross stitch kits for adults aren't just a form of self-care while you're doing them, but they can also turn into items you display and admire in the home.

Cross stitch is a type of sewing - it's a kind of counted thread embroidery using X-shaped stitches, typically on a fabric like linen or aida.

You can find a range of traditional or contemporary cross stitch kits for adults online - and it's definitely one of those 'cool kid' crafts that appeals to younger and older generations alike.

Cross stitch has many benefits for those who get into it: not only can it be relaxing and a wonderful hobby to focus on, it's a fantastic way to revamp old clothing or home furnishings for those looking to be more sustainable in their lifestyles.

Another benefit of cross stitch? You can enjoy de-stressing - and making something beautiful - on the cheap, with a selection of online kits starting at under a tenner.

One of the lovely bits about picking up a cross-stitch hobby is that you'll easily find a pattern you like, from florals and animals to words and phrases.
