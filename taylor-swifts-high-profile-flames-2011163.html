Taylor Swift's Dating History: Timeline of Famous Exes, Boyfriends

When it comes to a lover, you’ll never find another like Taylor Swift — and based on what her exes have said about her post-split, it sounds like they would agree.

Throughout her career, Swift has been linked to many A-List stars including Joe Jonas, Taylor Lautner, Harry Styles, Tom Hiddleston and others. While Swift has written many love songs about her former flames, they also have inspired her iconic breakup anthems, like, “Back to December” and “All Too Well.”

The Miss Americana star addressed her thoughts on what her exes think about her using their past in her music after rereleasing Red (Taylor’s Version) in November 2021.

“I haven’t thought about their experience, to be honest,” she said during an interview on Late Night with Seth Meyers ahead of the album’s release, which many fans connected back to her whirlwind connection to Jake Gyllenhaal.

The performer admitted that she was enjoying the 30-track release because the heartbreak that inspired it was long behind her.
