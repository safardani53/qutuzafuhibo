Jesse Palmer at the Walt Disney Television Upfront Presentation, Arrivals, Tavern on the Greenork on May 14, 2019. Jesse is a TV and sports commentator. He served as the backup quarterback for the New York Giants in the early aughts. He was cut by the team in 2005 and went on to play in the Canadian Football League before deciding to pursue a career in sports broadcasting in 2007. In 2004 he starred as season five’s ‘The Bachelor’ and offered his final rose to a blonde named Jessica, only to break up a few months later. In Sept. of 2017 he began hosting DailyMailTV. In July of 2019, he proposed to girlfriend of two years Emely Fardo in a romantic setting in Champagne, France. He presented her with a 3.5 carat diamond ring and planned the location specifically as it was the location of their first trip aborad as a couple after meeting in a New York City boxing class two years prior.
