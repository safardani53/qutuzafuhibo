Bachelor Father TV Star Dies At 72 Hollywood Life

Noreen died at Valley Presbyterian in Van Nuys, Calif., The Hollywood Reporter confirmed. She was 72. Her brother, Kevin Corcoran, who starred in the classic film Old Yeller, died in Oct. 2015.

Noreen rose to fame when she starred on Bachelor Father. She played Kelly, who goes to live with her uncle Bentley Gregg (John Forsythe), after her parents were killed in a car accident. She had been recommended by actor Ronald Reagan, who went on to become the 40th President of the United States.

The show ran from 1957 to 1962, totaling 157 episodes. Noreen transformed from a teenager to a sophisticated young woman. 

After Bachelor Father ended, she starred in films like Gidget Goes to Rome and The Girls on the Beach. She also appeared on TV shows such as Gunsmoke, The Big Valley, Dr. Kildare and Ben Casey. Noreen also recorded the single “Love Kitten.” She left her TV and film career behind for good in 1965. Noreen began an 11-year career with the renowned Lewitzky Dance Company.

Noreen never married. Her survivors include her two sisters, Donna and Kerry, and her niece. She will be missed.
