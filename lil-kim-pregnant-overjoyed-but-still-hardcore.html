Pregnant, Overjoyed, But Still Hardcore!

Lil Kim says she may be pregnant, but she is not going to be any less hardcore, even if her insane fashion choices need to take a backseat for awhile.

"I’m so excited, overjoyed! In heaven, waiting for my little sunshine," the 39-year-old told Us in a typically tight dress, which accentuated her growing belly.

"I can’t wear corsets and all my favorite sexy clothes [anymore]," she said. "But it’s a very beautiful thing and I’m so thrilled. Sweats, my comfy pajamas."

"Mainly my comfy loose fitting sweats … but you know they’re cute!"

While it’s not immediately clear who got Lil Kim pregnant, there are perks. Namely "Pizza! Everyday Pizza!" That does make being a pregnant Lil Kim seem fun.

 

Lil’ Kim performed "Lighters Up" for the crowd Wednesday night, reassuring Us that she’ll continue working even after her little bundle of joy arrives.

"I’m still going to be hardcore," she promised, adding:

"This baby has made me even more of a beast!"

Expect her to start going Rachel Frederickson soon too … well maybe not losing 60 percent of her body weight, but you get the idea. Working out hard.

"I think in another month or two I’ll start trying to exercise. My doctor is helping me through everything with being healthy and I feel so good right now."

"I’m still going to work. I’m going to be a working mom!"

Thank goodness.
