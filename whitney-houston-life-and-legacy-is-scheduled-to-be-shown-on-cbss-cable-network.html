Whitney Houston Life And Legacy Is Scheduled To Be Shown On CBSs Cable Network

Whitney Houston, A Look Back is a documentary on the life and impact of music diva Whitney Houston is due to premiere on CBS’s cable network. Whitney, a Look Back, produced by Entertainment Tonight for CBS network television, is expected to deliver audiences never-before-seen video and interviews with the legendary singer. The show, which will debut soon after the 10th anniversary of the singer’s death, will cover the legend’s life leading up to that awful day when the artist died.

More About Whitney Houston

Whitney Houston is a household name in the world of pop music. Houston was a singer and actress who sold more than 200 million albums over her career. She is most known for her famous songs, including I Wanna Dance with Somebody, Higher Love, and The Greatest Love of All. Houston received other honors as well. The vocalist has eight Grammys and two Emmys to her name. She has also appeared in films such as The Bodyguard in 1992 and the Cinderella remake in 1997.

The artist died in 2012 at the age of 48, leaving the world in a state of shock. Though she is no longer among us, her impact has persisted in music, movies, and pop culture. Following her tenth death anniversary, Entertainment Tonight will air a show titled Whitney, A Look Back, in her honor. The documentary will include a never-before-seen video from the Entertainment Tonight archives, as well as fresh interviews with luminaries such as Dionne Warwick, Clive Davis, CeCe Winans, Monica, and Kelly Price. The extra feature will also include some of Houston’s lost performances and unique moments.

When will Whitney, A Look Back be released? How do you watch it?

The Whitney Houston special is slated to air on CBS network television on April 2, 2022. Following its debut, the special will be available to watch online on Paramount Plus for members of the streaming service. Subscriptions begin at $4.99 per month. It is also available to watch on fuboTV, which offers subscription levels beginning at $64.99 per month. Aside from Whitney, A Look Back, a number of Houston-related projects are in the works, including a biopic about the singer starring Naomi Ackie, which is planned to air in December.
