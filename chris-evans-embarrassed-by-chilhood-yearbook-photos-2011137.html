Chris Evans Embarrassed by Childhood Yearbook Photos

He famously bulked up for his role in Captain America: The First Avenger, but Chris Evans wasn't always such a looker.

During a Tuesday appearance on Late Night with Jimmy Fallon, host Jimmy Fallon surprised the 30-year-old actor with a series of embarrassing yearbook photos from Evans' childhood.

"We started drifting into the bowl cut era," Evans said, referencing his 7th grade picture. "And the clear braces were something special."
