What is K. Michelle's net worth?

KIMBERLY Michelle Pate, who famously goes by the stage name K. Michelle, first rose to fame on VH1's Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta.

Since her breakout role, her career has only grown and so has her net worth.

Who is K. Michelle?

Born March 4, 1982, Pate, 39, is known as an American singer and television personality.

After appearing as a regular cast member on VH1's Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta for two seasons, her career took off and she even landed a record deal with Atlantic Records.

Her debut album, Rebellious Soul, was first released in 2013 and she has since released four additional studio albums and recorded over 25 music videos.

Prior to Atlantic Records, Pate was signed with Jive Records who produced her first single Fakin' It in 2009, which featured Missy Elliot.

Pate is often seen posting on Instagram to her 7.5million followers.

What is K. Michelle's net worth?

A career in the music industry often comes with a nice payday and Pate's career is no exception.

As of February 2022, she has an estimated net worth of $2.5million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

This number comes from not only her music career but also from starring on multiple TV shows.

Outside of Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta, Pate has also been seen on Love & Hip Hop: New York, K. Michelle: My Life, and Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood.

Pate is also the founder of the Atlanta, Georgia-based restaurant Puff and Petals, which opened in 2017.

Has K. Michelle had plastic surgery?

Over the years, Pate has been very open about her cosmetic surgeries, including the problems she has had from her illegal butt injections.

“Woman, I’m not playing with your body. I don’t care if you’re telling me that. Injections are illegal for a reason," she told fans in 2020.

Pate had to undergo reconstructive surgery to remove the black market hydrogel injections from her body.

Since 2018, she has been working on removing the silicone that was placed in her body and in 2021, was seen rocking her natural curves in a thong, free of any cosmetic changes.

At this time, it remains unclear if she has had any additional procedures.
