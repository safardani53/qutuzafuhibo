Shoppers rave over 99p Home Bargains Christmas scented sticks that make your tree smell beautiful

SHOPPERS are raving about Home Bargains 99p Christmas scented sticks, claiming that they make your home smell “beautiful.”

Taking to the Facebook group Extreme Couponing & Bargains UK one mum shared a snap of her purchase.

She had picked up three different scents including Gingerbread, Frosted Vanilla and Sugared Fig & Blackberry at under £1 each.

They are described as “ideal to hang on trees, wreaths and garlands” with the fragrance lasting up to 30 days.

Delighted with her purchase, she captioned her post: “99p each in home bargains.”

And she wasn’t the only fan, with her post receiving almost 2000 likes and hundreds of comments from fellow enthusiasts.

One wrote: “Last year we used them. They were amazing.”

Others were quick to share their tips on where to put the diffusers with some even popping them in their hoover.

“Your tree can not only be stunning but smell beautiful too 🎄😍 I have the gingerbread ones” wrote one.

Another added: “I use these in my hoover…smells lush.”

A third added: “I hang them in back of my radiators as well gives off the smell even more.”
