Ben Savage Got Married To His Long-Time Girlfriend Tessa Angermeier

Ben Savage and Tessa Angermeier got married. A picture on Instagram by one of the guests, Courtney O’Connell, verified the story. Several photos from the nuptials were included in the article, including two of Ben and Tessa dancing together:

“What a fantastic way to celebrate a couple of the decade!” Congratulations to the Savages!! #hellosavage”

The couple had been courting since 2018 and got engaged in January.

Tessa Angermeier is an artist.

Tessa Angermeier, 30, was born and raised in Owensville, Indiana. Her parents are the proprietors of KANT Brewing Co., a brewpub that opened in 2022. Tessa attended Indiana University, where she earned a degree in visual design as well as arts and music administration. She is presently employed as a senior graphic artist at Ben Soleimani, a well-known West Hollywood furnishings shop. She also works as a logo artist, marketing manager, and tour organizer for the band The Growlers. Tessa is also on Instagram, but her account is set to private. Details about her early life and work have yet to be disclosed.

Ben Savage is well-known for his roles in pictures and television shows.

Ben Savage made his television début as Matthew Lacey in five segments of Dear John, an NBC comedy. This was followed in 1990 by The Wonder Years. He starred as Chris Bankston in the NBC comedy A Family for Joe, which ran for one season and had nine episodes. He rose to prominence after portraying Cory Matthews in the ABC comedy Boy Meets World.

Little Monsters, Clifford, Car Babes, Palo Alto, Peace and Riot, and other features have featured the 42-year-old actor. He has appeared in a number of other TV series, including Maybe This Time, Party of Five, Still Standing, Phil of the Future, Chuck, Criminal Minds, Homeland, and others.

Ben Savage and Tessa Angermeier’s relationship

In 2018, Ben Savage and Tessa Angermeier began courting. Ben proposed to Tessa in January 2023 and shared a photo of the couple standing in front of a lake somewhere in Owensville, Indiana. The text read:

“I believe the best is yet to come.”

Following their proposal, Savage’s spokesperson stated that the two were thankful and enjoyed spending time with their family and friends. Despite being closely connected since 2018, the couple has chosen to keep their romance out of the spotlight. Savage attended the ribbon-cutting event when Tessa’s folks established a brewpub in 2022. Angermeier backed Savage’s choice to compete for Congress earlier this year, and she was photographed with Ben while he was campaigning for West Hollywood City Council in 2022.
