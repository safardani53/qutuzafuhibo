Anais Gallagher poses in saucy mesh two-piece for raunchy underwear brand

ANAIS Gallagher needs to strap in because things might be about to get tense.

The model, 20, posed in a mesh two-piece as part of her collaboration with underwear brand Bluebella - but is yet to show the shots to her father Noel.

She’s hoping the former Oasis rocker, 54, will see it as an ‘empowering’ experience for the student.

She said: “I haven’t told my dad yet! This will be the first time he has seen them - but he is very open about what I do.

“I think any father would be happy with this message of their daughter being empowered, and that’s exactly what this is.”

Anais, pronounced "Ah-nay-iss", was born on January 27, 2000, making her 20 years old.

Her dad is Oasis guitarist and songwriter Noel Gallagher, and her mum is Noel's interior designer ex-wife Meg Mathews.

Anais — who got her first "pony" aged seven — studied at posh school Bedales in Hampshire until she was 14, before moving to a school in Hampstead, London, near Primrose Hill.
