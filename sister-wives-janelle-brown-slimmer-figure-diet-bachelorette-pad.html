Sister Wives' Janelle Brown shows off her much-slimmer figure as she sips diet drink in kitchen of U

SISTER Wives star Janelle Brown has flaunted her slimmed-down figure while promoting a diet drink.

The TLC personality has lost more than 100 pounds since her split from ex-husband Kody Brown.

Janelle, 54, took to her Instagram feed to once again promote the product – Plexus – which she claims helped her achieve her weight loss goals.

Her co-star and former sister wife Christine Brown also swears by the beverage.

The Sister Wives star sat in front of her Christmas tree in a portion of the video.

Her festive decor was lit up with white lights and decorated with gold accents.

"With a wide range of products tailored to different needs, there’s something for everyone. Reach out to me for more information on these amazing products by messaging me @life_with_health_and_happiness and start your journey towards better health today!"

SISTER SLIMDOWN

Earlier this month, Janelle surprised fans by showing off her significantly slimmer face in a new video.

In the clip, she was seen sitting at a car dealership.

The reality star showed off her voluminous hairstyle and thin face.

She donned a beige jacket with a floral ensemble underneath.

The TLC star revealed that she had canceled her workout session due to a longer-than-expected visit to the dealership.

Janelle explained: "To give a little credit, my amazing service guy knew I was driving to Vegas, and they saw a nail in my tire and fixed it for free.

"And replaced the batteries in the key fob. So, the time wasn’t completely wasted."

Fans cared less about the long wait for her oil change, focusing instead on her weight loss.

"Hey, at least you look pretty while waiting!" one person responded.

Another gushed: "They want to keep you hostage. So they can enjoy your beauty longer!"

"Look at you! glowing!" a user shared.

Another reacted with: "You look amazing!! You look so relaxed and give off such good energy."

JANELLE'S JOURNEY

In November 2022, the U.S. Sun exclusively revealed that Janelle had slimmed down to nearly half her former size.

An insider said at the time: "Janelle has lost a ton of weight. She does not look the same. She's half the size she was."

Amid the slimdown, the TV star revealed the secret to her success.

In addition to dieting and eating healthy, she has been using Plexus Slim Pink Drink and the Plexus MetaBurn. 

Her weight loss journey appears to some fans to be linked to her split from Kody, 54.
