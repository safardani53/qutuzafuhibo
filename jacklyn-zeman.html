Jacklyn Zeman Age, Biography

Jacklyn Zeman was born on March 6, 1953, in Englewood, New Jersey, into a Jewish family. Her parents were Rita Zeman-Rohlman, a magazine supervisor, and Richard S. Zeman, a systems engineer. Jacklyn Lee Zeman was her full name. Zeman grew up in Bergenfield, New Jersey, and was of American nationality and white ethnicity.

Zeman graduated from Bergenfield High School at the age of 15 before pursuing a career in dance on a scholarship at New York University.
