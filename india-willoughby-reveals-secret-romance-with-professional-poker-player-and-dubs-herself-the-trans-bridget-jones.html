India Willoughby reveals 'secret romance with professional poker player' and dubs herself the 'trans

INDIA Willoughby has revealed she had a secret romance with a professional poker player.

The BBC newsreader, who fully transitioned three years ago, branded herself the “trans Bridget Jones” as she opened up about being unlucky in love.

According to the Daily Star, the 51-year-old opened up about her “amazing” night, saying: "I dated a professional poker player who I met online. He'd just won £100,000 and we had an amazing night out in Newcastle.”

She continued: "He wanted to see me again but I panicked.

"I'm the Bridget Jones of trans!"

During her time in CBB, India has made no secret of her desire to find love, admitting she doesn’t want to be “lonely”.

She sparked a huge row in the house when she quizzed singer Ginuwine about whether he would date a transgender person.

When he said no, she launched into a tirade and suggested he was transphobic.

She said: "The fact Ginuwine here is happy to go with women, but wouldn’t feel comfortable going with me....

"If you're in my situation trying to date, there's an extremely narrow view.
