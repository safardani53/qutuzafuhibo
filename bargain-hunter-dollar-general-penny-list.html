How does penny shopping work?

You only need one tool with you while penny shopping, your phone.

You need the Dollar General app to scan items to make sure you have the correct product and that it scans as one cent.

The most important rule, Taryn explained in the comments, is to not ask any workers about penny items.

Penny items are supposed to be discontinued items that are supposed to be pulled off the shelves.

Thefreebieguy on Instagram said to treat penny shopping as a scavenger hunt.

Look for the items and make sure to scan them with your phone on the app.

"You never really know that a penny item is a penny until the first person buys it in the morning," Taryn said in her TikTok.

"Before you head out, make sure you type some of these UPC numbers into the Dollar General app," she said.

What if you find a penny item?

She suggested not asking Dollar General employees about penny items because, she found, they won't tell you.

Taryn said some managers are "cool" about it and some aren't.

Plus, if you find a penny item, Taryn said don't feel greedy, take them all.

She added that's because a Dollar General employee has the right to ask you where you found it and can pull the rest off the shelves.

In the end, when you are looking for penny items, Taryn said you need to look high and low, move things around but always clean up after yourself.
