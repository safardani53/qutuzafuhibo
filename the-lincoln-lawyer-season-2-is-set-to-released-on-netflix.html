The Lincoln Lawyer Season 2 Is Set To Released On Netflix

The Lincoln Lawyer, a riveting legal drama series, is set to return to Netflix in the autumn of 2023 with its much anticipated second season. The Netflix drama series, developed by David E. Kelley, has its own fan following after season 1 earned a lot of great reviews from both the public and the reviewers for its extremely captivating plots and remarkable acting performances by the key cast members.

Fans of the series have undoubtedly been anticipating how season 2 would develop, particularly given the previous season’s dramatic chain of events. The Lincoln Lawyer season 2 latest release date and additional information revealed ahead of its Netflix launch]

What can fans anticipate from Season 2 of the series?

The successful series, based on Michael Connelly’s acclaimed novel The Brass Verdict, has been renewed for a second season, which will premiere on June 14, 2022. Season 2 shooting is slated to begin in late October 2022 and conclude on March 23, 2023. Author Michael Connelly went to Instagram in November 2022 to declare that shooting for the second season has commenced. He said in an Instagram post:

“From the set of Season 2 of The Lincoln Lawyer: I’m happy to say that season 2 has begun shooting. It feels great to be back on set. This year, we are adapting The Fifth Witness, and we have some incredible new cast, crew, and writing team members. It’s going to be fantastic! – MC”

As a result, the next season of the legal drama series will be published between October and November 2023. Season 2 of the highly anticipated series will be based on Michael Connelly’s fourth novel in the Mickey Haller book series, The Fifth Witness. Ted Humphrey, who developed the first season, will also serve as showrunner for the second. Despite the fact that no official summary for the upcoming season has been posted, Humphrey said on Twitter:

“We’re so excited to welcome back so many familiar characters in Season 2!”

It has also been reported that Rob Seidenglanz would direct the premiere episode of the second season. Fans’ favorite criminal-defense lawyer Mickey Haller will return in the upcoming season to take on a brand-new and extremely fascinating case. As a result, it will be exciting to watch where the upcoming season will take the series.

All About The Lincoln Lawyer season 2 cast

Manuel Garcia-Rulfo, the series’ primary actor, will reprise his eponymous role as Mickey Haller. However, Neve Campbell, who portrays Maggie McPherson in the series, will be featured as a recurrent actress rather than a series regular in season 2. Season 2’s cast includes several promising talents such as Yaya DaCosta as Andrea “Andy” Freeman, Lana Parrilla as Lisa Trammell, and Matt Angel as Henry Dahl.
