Who Is Rojean Kar? Travis Scott Denies Cheating On Kylie With IG Model Hollywood Life

  • Travis Scott denied he cheated on Kylie Jenner with Rojean Kar
  • Rojean disputed Travis’s claims that he didn’t know who she was.
  • Rojean Kar is an Instagram model.

“A lot of weird sh-t going on,” Travis Scott posted on Oct. 23, amid a round of rumors that he cheated on Kylie Jenner with Instagram model Rojean Kar. After Rojean posted an Instagram Story of her seemingly hanging out on Travis’s production set, the 31-year-old rapper took to his social media to shut down the buzzing over any alleged infidelity. “An uninvited person was sneaking photos on what was supposed to be a closed set what I was directing a video,” wrote Travis. “I’m saying this for the last time: I don’t know this person. I’ve never been with this person. So please stop with the continuous cyber games and the fictional storytelling.”

After a short break apart, Travis and Kylie rekindled their love in 2021 as they welcomed their second child together. Since then, things have been going strong, with the two going on date nights together. So, who is this person that Travis claims he doesn’t know? Who is Rojean Kar?

Rojean Refuted Travis’s Denial

 “What we’re not gonna do is we’re not gonna lie on me,” Rojean said in a series of videos posted to her Instagram Story, per Cosmopolitan. “I pretended I didn’t know you, went along with whatever f-cking narrative… no matter how much bullsh-t I got from it. But to say you don’t know me and you’ve never been with me when you’ve definitely been with me, when everybody’s seen you with me, when I have pictures and videos of you with me? Come on. Come on, sir.”

“I ran out the f-cking door, and you had every single girl I know blowing me up like, ‘Travis is asking for you. Come back.’ Are we pretending that didn’t happen, too? You cheat on that b-tch  every single f-cking night. The whole f-cking city sees it! Don’t do this. … I don’t care. I hope your PR stunt-happy family sh-t goes well, and the whole world buys it, even though you’re the sh-ttiest human being who has ever walked this earth.” She then alluded to having “a bunch of naked videos and pictures” of Travis.

Rojean Kar Is An Instagram Model

Rojean, aka @YungSweetRo on Instagram, is a social media personality. As of October 2022, she had over 382k followers on Instagram. Like most in her profession, she posts photos of herself in sexy and glamorous outfits.

She Likes To Travel

In March and June 2022, Rojean posted a series of snaps from her separate trips to Paris. “et la chanson que tu chantais.. toujours, toujours je l’entendrai,” she captioned her March gallery. She has also posted photos from her travels to sunnier locations (which have provided her with opportunities to model her collection of bikinis.)

She Was Previously Linked To Travis Scott

Rojean and Travis were first reportedly linked in 2013, per Page Six, and some fans have wondered if Travis ever fully ended things with her. When Travis and Kylie paused their romantic relationship in 2019, there were rumors that Rojean caused the break. She later denied any involvement. “None of these rumors are true, it’s just the internet creating a false narrative,” she wrote on an Instagram Story, per E! News. “Please stop spreading lies & leave him, her & I alone because it’s affecting real lives. Thank you.”

Rojean Kar Attended Kanye West’s Concert In December 2021

“I missed shows so much that I didn’t even act like I’m too cool to take videos,” Rojean captioned an IG gallery she posted on Dec. 11, 2021, showing her time at Kanye “ye” West and Drake’s Free Larry Hoover Benefit Concert. Rojean shared photos and videos from what looked to be the pre-show setup, including an apparent soundcheck from Ye himself. She did capture a moment when Drake and Ye were onstage together, as well as a shot of the crowd at the LA Memorial Coliseum.
