Kady McDermott shows off incredible figure in cut-out mini dress

KADY McDermott stunned as she sported an array of glittering outfits.

The reality TV star, who rose to fame on Love Island, took to Instagram to pose in a variety of party-ready ensembles.

Snapping several clips on her stories, Kady could be seen sharing her "Christmas and New Years Eve party outfits" in a walk-through "try-on haul".

The first look was a black and silver two piece, including a tube top and a figure-hugging skirt, encrusted with sequins.

She then rocked a strappy, black mini-dress with tassels and silver sparkles - perfect for dancing.

But the star of the show had to be a sequinned silver dress with cut-outs and rose-shaped embellishing.
