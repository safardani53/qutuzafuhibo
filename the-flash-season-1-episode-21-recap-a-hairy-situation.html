The Flash Season 1 Episode 21 Recap: A Hairy Situation

Barry Allen found himself in a hairy situation on The Flash Season 1 Episode 21.

Get it? Because he did battle with a giant gorilla??? Trust us. That’s funny.

Watch The Flash Season 1 Episode 21 Online

But while the initial appearance of Grodd was pretty darn cool, we were left with a slightly bitter taste in our Flash-loving mouths this week considering the beast was felled simply by a passing train.

It would have been nice to see the team actually come up with a plan to take Grodd down, as challenging as that would have been.

Elsewhere, a majority of this installment was dedicated to Iris. She knows the truth about Barry, of course, and finally confronted him about it inside S.T.A.R. Labs.

Hi, Barry. Or should I say, The Flash?

Can’t get much more straightforward than that, can you?

Barry tried to come out with a retort after Iris accused him of lying (about EVERYthing), saying she claimed at Christmas that she didn’t share his romantic feelings and, hey, wasn’t that also a lie?!?

We’re not sure if that’s really a comparable lie, but at least the guy is trying.
