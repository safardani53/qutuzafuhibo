McDonald's just brought back its beloved Happy Meal toy 20 years later and fans are asking if 'it's

MCDONALD'S fans have shared their excitement after noticing the fast food giant had brought back a happy meal favorite after two decades.

A recent leak has revealed that McDonald's is bringing back their Crash Bandicoot toys, a fan favorite that has not been distributed by the restaurant in 20 years.

Last month, an X user, formerly known as Twitter, gave fans a sneak peek of the toys the fast food chain planned to release.

"Here’s a leak of Crash Bandicoot coming to McDonald’s! Who is excited for when this comes in December!? #CrashBandicoot" they claimed.

The photo showed a card board with a few of the plushes as well as photos of the other toys.

One user replied: "Is that for real?!!!"

Another begged: "Is it Real? Or Fake?"

The brand plans to dish out the following 8 items:

  • Crash Collector Card Game
  • Crash’s Memory Card Game
  • Spyro’s Memory Card Game
  • Crash and Dingodile Plush
  • Coco and Spyro Plush
  • Stack-n-Crash Challenge
  • Speed Jigsaw Puzzle
  • Tawna’s Tic-Tac-Toe Game

The childhood favorite has recently made a comeback for children with its multiplayer video game that was released in June of this year.

Fans in the US can get their toys now, with a purchase of Happy Meal.

This will be the third release with the restaurant, with Crash Bandicoot making its first appearance with the brand in 2004, Dexerto reported.

At the time McDonald's had released six handheld games that featured the characters on them.

They would make their second appearance in 2005, releasing a new set of handheld games for kids to enjoy.

This is not the only nostalgic treat McDonald is giving its customers.

The company recently teased the comeback of its snack wraps.

Snack wraps were originally introduced in 2006, and were staples for the company until they took them off the menu in 2016.

However, In a press release, the company announced its plan to expand its chicken initiatives, which "includes plans to offer McCrispy in nearly all markets around the world by the end of 2025 and to expand McCrispy into wraps and tenders."

Fans took to X to share their excitement.

One fast foodie wrote: "THE FUTURE MIGHT NOT BE SO BLEAK. Apparently, @McDonalds is bringing back the Snack Wrap! I might cry."

Another tweet reads: "Yooo @McDonalds I see you're clapping back @BurgerKing for coming after your snack wrap customers. But we are pumped they're back. Bada bop bop ba."

A third tweeted: "Is it true @McDonalds?!? Are yall bring back the chicken snack wrap??!!! Please don't let me down."

Despite the small tease, fans have not yet got a date for when the snack wrap will arrive on the menu again.
