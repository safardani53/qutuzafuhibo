Ben Affleck appears to have dyed his hair and beard darker as he walks dogs in LA The US Sun

BEN Affleck appears to have dyed his hair and beard darker as he ran errands in Los Angeles.

The Batman actor had noticeably darker hair as took his and girlfriend Ana de Armas' dogs out for a stroll.

Ben, 47, and Ana walked both his German Shepherd Hutch alongside the Knives Out actress' adorable Maltese, Elvis.

He wore dark wash jeans with a white and red T-shirt.

With only his aviator glasses, he displayed his full, darker hair, compared to a more salt-and-pepper look in April.

Ana, 32, wore a floral summer dress as she clung onto her boyfriend's arm.

Ben met Ana last fall on the set for film Deep Water.

His relationship with Ana has been going from strength to strength, and even introduced her to kids, Violet, 13, Seraphina, 11,, and Samuel, eight, who he shares with with ex Jennifer Garner.

Ben's dad Timothy recently told The Sun he's looking forward to meeting his son's "splendid" girlfriend.

He told us: "I haven't met Ana yet, but I'm looking forward to it, from everything I've heard she's a very splendid woman and I'm glad they're together...

"Especially in times like these, you need good company, it's not healthy to be alone I don't think, so it's good he's got a partner, that's important, it's good timing."

He added: "I don't follow the internet stuff, but I'm sure, knowing Ben as well as I do, he's always had really interesting, nice girlfriends and I'm sure Ana's one of them."
