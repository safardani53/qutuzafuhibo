How to apply for The Chase on ITV application form, criteria and deadline

EVER been sat watching The Chase on ITV and thought to yourself, "I could do that'?

Well, why not give it a go? If you think you've got it takes to take on The Chasers at the famous ITV studios, here's what you need to know about the application process...

How do I apply to be on The Chase?

Do you have what it takes to take on The Beast, The Governess and The Dark Destroyer?

ITV’s The Chase is looking for people who can ace general knowledge questions, play their tactics right and win a cash pot worth thousands of pounds.

Presenter Bradley Walsh is on hand to help if the nerves get the better of you.

The daytime programme is currently taking applications for series 13 – but contestants must be 18 or older.

You can apply to be on the show by clicking here.

Good luck!

When is the Chase on ITV?

The Chase is back on our screens Monday to Friday.

It is shown at 5pm on ITV every weekday.

It goes head-to-head with Pointless on BBC One, Flog It! on BBC Two and Four in a Bed on Channel 4

What is The Chase?

The teatime game show sees four plucky contestants go up against one of Britain’s best quiz brains in a bid to win thousands of pounds.

The teams compete against the chaser, one of six know-it-all quizzers, in a bid to keep the money that they win.

The Chase has amassed a huge cult following and is ITV's most successful daytime show ever.
