Who is Waka Flocka Flame?

Born in New York, New York, on May 31, 1986, Waka Flocka Flame is a well-known rapper whose real name is Juaquin James Malphurs.

Waka became a mainstream artist after signing with 1017 Brick Squad and Warner Bros. Records in 2009.

Subsequently, he started gaining media attention after h released singles such as Hard in da Paint, No hands, and O Let's Do It.

In 2010, he released his debut studio album, Flockaveli.

Later in 2012, he released his second studio album, Triple F Life: Friends, Fans, and Family.

Since then he has released other hit songs to his credit.

He has also starred in television shows and films such as Waka & Tammy: What The Flocka, Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta, Black Dynamite, and a few others.
