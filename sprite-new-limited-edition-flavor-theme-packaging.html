Sprite launches new limited-edition flavor - customers will love the theme packaging on store

SPRITE has announced its newest limited-edition flavor in an effort to celebrate 50 years of hip hop.

The new flavor, Sprite Lymonade Legacy, comes with the same traditional lemon-lime taste but with a hint of lemonade, and it contains zero caffeine.

In a nod to the celebration, the packaging will feature boomboxes, mixers, and studio equipment.

Back in 1986, the partnership between hip-hop and Sprite started as the brand was the first major company to launch a national TV ad featuring hip-hop star Kurtis Blow.

“For the last 40 years, we’ve stayed committed to Hip-Hop and its endless possibilities,” Sprite said on its website. 

“Now, we’re celebrating 50 years of the genre alongside some of the greats with a series of limited-run drops that put Hip-Hop history in your hands,” the soda company added.

HOW TO ENTER THE SWEEPSTAKES

Each drop begins at 9am PT and ends at 8.59pm PT starting on May 15, 2023, through August 27, 2023.

During the Sweepstakes Period, look for special Sweepstakes displays or packaging with a QR code.

Customers can scan the QR code with their phones and be taken to the Sprite website.

During each Sweepstakes Period, head to www.sprite.com/drops.

Just note that there is no purchase required to enter and any purchase does not increase your chances of winning.

The Sweepstakes is only available to legal residents of the US.

Plus, all participants must be 18 or older as of the date of entry.

For a full list of drop dates and prizes, head to the Sprite website.
