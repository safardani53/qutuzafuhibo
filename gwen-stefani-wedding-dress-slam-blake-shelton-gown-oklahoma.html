Gwen Stefani's wedding dress revealed as fans slam Blake Shelton's wife's 'disappointing' gown after

GWEN Stefani’s wedding dress she wore in the Oklahoma ceremony with husband Blake Shelton has been revealed - leaving many fans “disappointed.” 

The No Doubt rocker traded her typically loud-patterned clothing for a strapless all-white ball gown complete with a multi-layer tulle skirt. 

The Voice coach’s long veil matched her poofy dress - with the fabric trailing feet behind her in the extended dress train. 

While many fans thought Gwen looked “gorgeous,” some are disappointed with the choice of wedding day attire.

In photos obtained by TMZ, the singer held her arms up in celebrations wearing her designer gown - with her beloved dog Betty hiding underneath layers of the sheer fabric.

“I’m not liking Gwen Stefani’s wedding dress and surprised she chose that,” one person tweeted. 

Another insisted: “Gwen’s style is never low key but she did wear a traditional (for her) white strapless multi layered tulle ball gown. 

“I prefer the satin organza hand dipped cream & pink dress she married Gavin Rossdale in.”

A third decided: ”Gwen Stefani marrying Blake Shelton is not punk rock."

“Gwen Stefani's recent wedding dress is fine. Conventional Cinderella. Given how ICONIQUE her previous dress was, she was never going to top it anyway. Looks lovely,” someone else wrote. 

On Monday, Gwen took to Instagram to share moments from her wedding to Blake.

In the snaps, the newlyweds posed outdoors and in a limo.

The final photo showed Blake and Gwen kissing as they stood near the wedding cake.

Alongside the photos, the singer wrote: "July 3rd 2021 dreams do come! @blakeshelton i love you."

The couple's ceremony was officiated by The Voice host Carson Daly.

The No Doubt singer’s recent wedding to Blake wasn’t her first, as she previously married singer Gavin Rossdale, 55, in 2002.

At her first wedding, Gwen wore a custom Christian Dior gown designed by John Galliano. 

The wedding dress included classic elements, as well as noticeable volume and a bottom that was dip-dyed hand-painted hot pink.

The rockers- who share sons Kingston, 15, Zuma, 12, and Apollo, seven, together- split in 2016.

The two Voice coaches tied the knot over Fourth of July weekend, while they had previously applied for their marriage license on Tuesday in Oklahoma.

Blake and Gwen had planned on walking down the aisle just about 10 days from the date they filed for a marriage license, according to the Johnston County Court Clerk documents obtained by TMZ.

The lavish ceremony took place on the country singer’s sprawling 1,200-acre ranch.
