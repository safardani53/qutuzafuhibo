London travel chaos as Rotherhithe Tunnel CLOSED in both directions after three-car pile up The

ROTHERHITHE Tunnel has been closed this morning after a horrific three-car pile up.

Shocking photos show debris scattered through the tunnel, with at least two cars badly damaged.

One man, believed to be in his 30s, was rushed to hospital where he remains in a critical condition.

Transport for London warned this morning the tunnel had been closed off in both directions.

They said: "Rotherhithe Tunnel is closed in both directions due to a collision.

"The tunnel is expected to remain closed during peak morning hours for traffic.

"Use alternative routes, expect heavy traffic on approaches."

The crash involving the three cars unfolded just after 2am.

Met Police confirmed police and ambulance crews had rushed to the scene.

Closures remain in place while the scene is dealt with, a spokesman said.
