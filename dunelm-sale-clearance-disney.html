Dunelm launches huge clearance sale with cleaning supplies and homeware starting from 1

WITH Christmas over for another year, many of us want to save a few pennies.

This hasn’t stopped shoppers from racing to Dunelm though, with home wear on offer for just £1 during the shop’s clearance sale.

Though you might feel like you can’t splash the cash right now, there is something for everyone in the huge sale.

Unlike other outlets, Dunelm have reduced thousands of branded items in their sale – all retailing for up 50 per cent off right now.

Fans of the home wear specialist first noticed the huge discounts last week, with eagled eyed shoppers sharing their finds in the Extreme Couponing and Bargains group on Facebook.

Among the endless products on offer were adorable Disney memorabilia, including an adorable Mickey Mouse sharing plate – down from £7 to £4

Whether you are hoping to make yours or your little ones rooms more magical, there’s plenty to choose from with a Disney themed lamp shade now on sale for just £7.50.

You can even added to your mug stash, with crockery from the House of Mouse starting from as little as £1.70 per cup or £3.50 for an extra-large.

However, there is even more in store for bargain hunters as Dunelm have also heavily discounted their bedding, with a tropical double duvet set now only £15.

There is a lot on offer for nature lovers, as there is bedding from the Natural History Museum on sale too.

If want to give David Attenborough a run for his money, now is your chance as the meadow double duvet sets are just £14 each.

It’s not just smaller home wear that has been dramatically reduced though, with Dunelm also rolling back the cost of large furniture too.

For those hoping to save on your energy bills, you can pick up electric blankets in the sale for just a fraction of their normal cost.

Rather than spending over £70 to keep cosy, Dunelm’s own brand blankets are now just £17.50 for a single.

With these low prices, you can even upgrade to a double with the large blanket costing under £25.

Dunelm have even more ways to keep cosy, with fluffy rugs also being massively discounted in the sale and costing as little as £27.30

Once you’ve finished furnishing your home, you also won’t have to spend a lot keeping it clean either.

The home wear shop has also many cleaning products in the sale - including the Vileda Turbo Smart Spin Mop (£20).

If you weren’t already sold, the sale is also available online with Dunelm's website having items for £10 or under - meaning you won’t even have to go outside to back a bargain.
