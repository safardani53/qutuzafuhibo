I got a cheap tattoo on holiday in Ayia Napa and it's a big regret... it looks like a wonky banana

A YOUNG woman regretted getting a new tattoo on holiday in Cyprus after it came out looking like a "wonky banana".

Jessica Jones posted a video on TikTok showing the disappointing tattoo she ended up with.

Jessica had been hoping for a small tattoo of a moon.

But she described what she got as nothing but a "wonky banana".

She captioned her video: "I'd never get a cheap tattoo in Ayia Napa."

TikTok users agreed that the tattoo was not a success.

One said: "Where did you go because I will not be going there."

Another said: "Glad I didn’t come with you now."

It comes after a Harry Styles fan was left stunned when a tattoo artist decided to put a unique spin on her tattoo tribute to the pop star.

Angela was hoping for a pretty simple inking of a fish inside a coffee pot, inspired by Styles’ Adore You Music Video.

However, she ended up sobbing when she spotted the ‘awful’ finished product - and now people are suggesting the tattooist ‘dropped the ball hard’. 

An expert recently offered advice to people thinking of having their first tattoo.

The tattoo ink artist said that many people chose the wrong design , which led to a bad tattoo.

The artist also said that some people chose the wrong part of the body for the tattoo to be drawn.

The artist advised against tattoos in highly visible places such as the face.

Many people regret tattoos when they are older in life, which are deemed to be embarrassing in front of new partners and colleagues.
