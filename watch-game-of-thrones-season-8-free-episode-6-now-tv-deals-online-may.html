What is Now TV?

Like Netflix or Amazon Video, Now TV is a telly streaming service that lets you watch TV shows and movies.

It's owned by Sky, and also features a host of Sky's best live channels – including Sky Atlantic, which is where you'll find Game of Thrones.

Now TV is designed to be a no-contract alternative to Sky, that you can pay for on a monthly basis and cancel at any time.

And you buy select packages – like Entertainment, Cinema, Kids or Sport – so you only need to pay for the exact content type you want.

Now TV can be accessed on many TVs, consoles, phones, tablets and set-top boxes – as well as by purchasing a Now TV box or streaming stick.
