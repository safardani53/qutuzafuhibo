JIM Bob and Michelle Duggar’s children snubbed their famous parents as the couple celebrated their 36th wedding anniversary on Wednesday.

The parents have been having issues with some of their children as they decide to pull away from the family.

Jim Bob, 55, posted a sweet throwback photo of him and Michelle, 53, after they had exchanged vows 36 years ago.

"36 years ago today, at 10am, I married Michelle Ruark, my best friend!," the Counting On patriarch shared on social media.

"I am so thankful for Michelle. She is such an incredibly loving wife and mother, and a virtuous woman who has a ministry mindset and a gracious spirit!

"She is beautiful inside and out!! Her life has impacted the world. I love you always, Michelle! -Jim Bob."

Out of the 19 children, only two commented on the post wishing their parents a happy anniversary.

Their 23-year-old son, Josiah Duggar, commented: "Happy Anniversary Mom and Pops!
"Thank you for your dedication to each other, and for setting such a wonderful example of a Godly and strong marriage!"

Joseph Duggar, 25, also congratulated them: "Happy anniversary momma and pops.

"We are so blessed and grateful to have you as our parents! Love y'all so much!"

However, the Counting On star's other children completely ignored their parent's anniversary on social media.

Jill Duggar, on the one hand, even took the time to promote her new partnership with ModLi.

Along with several pictures of her in a conservative bathing suit, she wrote about how "excited" she was with the brand and even did a video reviewing the company.

Fans recently speculated Jill and Amy Duggar have been doing their own spinoff show as they spend more time together and pull away from their parents.

Jill and her husband Derick Dillard have broken free from the family and their conservative values, with Derick recently claiming the patriarch Jim Bob controls the finances of all the children.

Amy is also considered the black sheep of the family as her Christian values are more liberal than that of Jim Bob and his wife, Michelle.

Amy and Jill are incredibly close and shared a picture of a couples date night at a local Whiskey bar, Wellington, in Springdale, Arkansas.

However, the rest of the bar was entirely empty, which led to some fans wondering if they were filming a new series.

"Wouldn't it be something if Amy and Jill - or, I guess their headships, really - are negotiating their own spinoff for TLC, titled FREEDOM?" speculated one Reddit user.
