12 Best Reusable Breast Pads 2022 | The Sun UK

BREASTFEEDING is one of the best ways to feed babies in their first year of life and beyond. Breast milk contains a healthy balance of vitamins, nutrients and hormones that will help your newborn stay healthy.

However, it comes with its fair share of aches, pains and leakages, which is where the best reusable breast pads come in super handy.

Disposable, often plastic, breast pads aren't very environmentally friendly compared to long-lasting, budget-friendly reusable breast pads, which can be washed and reused time and time again.

Reusable pads are typically made of bamboo or cotton, although you'll also find silicone pads, which do more than catch leaks; they also prevent them.

They tend to be soft and ultra-absorbent, and most come with laundry bags so you can easily throw them in the washing machine without losing any in the process. Some also come in a variety of colours, prints and playful shapes, which may not sound essential, but does make the whole 'leaking bodily fluids' thing easier to cope with, somehow.

Where can I buy reusable breast pads?

Thankfully, you can buy reusable breast pads from a range of places - all according to your needs, and budget.

You can buy them from high-street shops like Boots and on handmade online gift shops like Etsy.

Amazon has the biggest range, though, and you can search according to design, needs and price-points.

How many reusable breast pads do you need per day?

The amount of reusable breast pads you need per day depends entirely on the wearer - you may find that you leak more overnight, than you do during the day, but everybody is different.

Mums usually recommend owning about six breast pads, at first - four for during the day, plus an extra pair for emergencies, or in the wash at any given time.

How often do you wash reusable breast pads?

For hygiene purposes, you should wash your reusable breast pads after every single use.

Wash according to packet instructions, often on a 30 or 40 degree wash - usually in the provided wash bag, to extend lifespan and to make sure you don't lose them.
