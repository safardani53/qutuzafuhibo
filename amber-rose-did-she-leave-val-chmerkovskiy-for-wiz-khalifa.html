Did She Leave Val Chmerkovskiy for Wiz Khalifa?!

Amber Rose sure moves fast, huh?

Remember how yesterday we heard that she and Val Chmerkovskiy had broken up?

After several months of dating, and after they spoke so fondly of each other just a couple of weeks ago, reports surfaced that they were over.

So far, we don’t have a real reason for the split, but it seems amicable enough — both Val and Amber have left all their sweet photos up on their social media accounts.

Though Val has been sending out some cryptic tweets that may or may not be about Amber.

On Friday night, he tweeted “If you’re ready, it’s too late,” which means … something, we’re sure.

And last night, he tweeted that “crying with laughter” emoji without context, which seems a little shady.

But after checking in on what Amber got up to last night, it looks like perhaps there was a bit of context.

Because last night, Amber attended the Clive Davis Pre-Grammy Gala with her ex-husband, Wiz Khalifa.

And they looked pretty darn friendly.

Not only did they walk the red carpet together, but they also kissed each other. On the lips.

In front of cameras.

Is it normal to just casually kiss your ex-husband, the father of your child? Totally platonic, no romantic feelings whatsoever, just a little kiss?

A kiss that was described by people witnessed it as lasting at least seven seconds?

It’s hard to say, but we’re going to go ahead and go with “nah.”

Another interesting thing about her little outing with Wiz is that giant ring on her left hand. See it up there?

It really is huge, isn’t it? It’s a gemstone surrounded by diamonds — it’s not her original engagement ring from Wiz, but could it be another engagement ring?

Sure, why not?

Amber and Wiz might not be re-engaged, but it wouldn’t be surprising at all if they’re back together.

When their divorce was finalized last year, they went out to a strip club together.

Afterwards, Amber tweeted “Too much fun with my Ex hubby last night & no we didn’t celebrate our divorce we celebrated our love 4 each other cuz that never goes away.”

She’s also said that “Wiz will always be my best friend, forever,” and that “He’s the love of my life, that’ll never, ever go away. But it just couldn’t work out that way.”

She added — and this is the real kicker — that “Maybe in a couple years we’ll get back together and have some more babies.”

So hey, maybe they’re just running a little ahead of schedule, Val Chmerkovskiy be damned?

It’s certainly beginning to look that way.
