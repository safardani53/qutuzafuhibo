ASHLEY Graham has shown off her curves in a sexy black sheer dress while attending the Barbie movie premiere.

The model wowed in the tulle polka-dot gown at the event in Los Angeles on Sunday - but admitted to suffering what she called an awkward AF wardrobe malfunction.

Ashley - who was crowned the World's Sexiest Woman by Maxim - wore a strapless dress with a fishtail skirt and heavily ruffled bodice.

The Sports Illustrated model teamed the skintight garment with a pair of towering black heels.

While the mother of three looked stunning in her eye-catching outfit, she admitted that she could barely sit down in it.

Ashley shared a video of herself lying awkwardly to the side in the backseat of a car on the way to the premier.
