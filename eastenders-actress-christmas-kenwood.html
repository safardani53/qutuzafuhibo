EastEnders star looks worlds away from Walford with husband and adorable baby at Christmas At Kenwoo

EASTENDERS actress Louisa Lytton beamed last night as she posed with her husband Ben Bhanvra and their adorable baby Aura at the launch of Christmas At Kenwood.

The Ruby Allen actress, 33, took to her Instagram Stories to share photos of the spectacular light show.

The pair wrapped up in winter coats and hats with sweet Aura sporting a purple ski suit, a pink beanie hat and suede booties.

The trio stopped for a photo outside Kenwood House which was illuminated in purple light.

The iconic grounds - used in Hugh Grant and Julia Roberts' romantic movie Notting Hill - were transformed into a dazzling light trail.

EastEnders favourite Louisa posted one memorable snap of Ben with Aura on his shoulders as they stood in front of a fairylight tree.

Louisa and Ben married in a stunning Italian ceremony in July after delaying their wedding three times due to Covid.

The soap star recently became just as emotional as she broke down as she walked into her daughter's stunning first birthday party.

She called in the professionals to bring the party to life with bespoke boxes that said "Aura is 1" for the kids and treats decorated with Aura's name.

The actress and her husband pulled out all the stops for Aura, putting on a lavish food and balloon display too.

Louisa recently returned to work on EastEnders but admitted it has left her "sick with guilt".

"I feel sick with guilt for going to work," she said on Instagram during a night away filming on location.

"So silly I know. I've brought a whole book and I intend on finishing it."

Later in the day, the BBC star took a selfie while travelling on London's new Elizabeth Line Crossrail service.

She admitted: "Just me crying on the Elizabeth Line. It's not even a week.

"I woke at 6am. Apparently Aura is still asleep at home. Ben has lucked out not me!"
