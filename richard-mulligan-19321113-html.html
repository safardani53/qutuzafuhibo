Richard Mulligan is an American television, film and character best known for his role as Burt Campbell, the loving and protective husband of Cathryn Damon's character, in the sitcom Soap. Mulligan then played Dr. Harry Weston in the Empty Nest, in which he won both the Emmy and Golden Globe Awards for Best Actor in a comedy series in 1989. Mulligan is the brother of director Robert Mulligan.
