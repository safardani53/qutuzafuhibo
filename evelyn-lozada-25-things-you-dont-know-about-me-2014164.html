Evelyn Lozada: 25 Things You Don't Know About Me

These days, Basketball Wives star Evelyn Lozada keeps scoring slam dunks — on top of starring in the hit VH1 show and running her own makeup line (Vida Lux Cosmetics), she also welcomed a baby boy last month. Now, she's doing a 60-Day weight loss challenge with youthH2O and GNC to lose the baby weight. Below, the 38-year-old reality star shares 25 things you don't know about her with Us Weekly. 

1. I've always wanted to do a fitness show.

2. I had an obsession with Wonder Woman & Barbie growing up.

3. I hate the smell of mustard.

4. When I was younger, I had 10 hamsters.

5. I didn't start drinking water until I was 25. 

6. Jennifer Lopez and I went clubbing together in New York. 

7. I'm afraid of the ocean, but will still go in.

8. I'm obsessed with CNN.

9. I have 150 perfumes.

10. My favorite food is Spanish.

11. I can eat avocado every day.

12. My favorite clothing designer is Roberto Cavalli.

13. I'm obsessed with anything that has a print.

14. I like drinking Coconut Ciroc on the rocks.

15. I’m obsessed with Vida Lux lip-gloss.

16. I love fitted ball caps.

17. My favorite meal of the day is breakfast.

18. I love weight training.

19. When I was younger I was terrified of fireworks.

20. As a kid, I loved eating mayonnaise and jelly on Italian bread.

21. I love to dance salsa and merengue.

22. I love roller coasters and theme parks. 

23. I was in a girl group with five or six girls in the neighborhood when I was a kid.  My stage name was Diamond and I wore jelly shoes.
