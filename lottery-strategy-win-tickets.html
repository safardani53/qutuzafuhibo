Im a lottery expert my strategy and how I win off tickets, you don't have to spend a lot

PLAYING the lottery is all about luck.

However, some players claim to have winning strategies.

TikToker Laura Diaz is one of those winners.

Laura, who goes by entrepreneur_queen on TikTok, has been playing scratchers for years and winning tons of cash along the way.

Her TikTok is filled with videos of her scoring $50, $500, and even $1,000 jackpots.

Though winning may ultimately come down to luck, some strategy is involved.

In a recent TikTok, Laura responded to a comment claiming that because she keeps winning, she must buy tons of tickets.

Her response was, "not exactly."

In this video, she used her winnings her from the last time she played.

She said that the week before she started with $40.

She then won $60 bought three more tickets and won $150.

So after winning last week, she started the process over once more to show her followers how she plays.

She repeated the same process and started off playing with just $40.

Her strategy

Laura said in order to win, she tends to choose tickets that attract her - typically by color or some other fun factor.

This time, she decided to choose the $20 Millionaire Bucks, a $10 Mystery Crossword, and another $10 California Dreamin’.

Out of those three purchases, she won something on two tickets.

After her winnings from both separate occasions, she was able to profit $150.

Other lottery strategies

Even though your chances of winning the lottery are low, there are still ways you can beat the odds.

In another video, Laura revealed a theory she’s heard and did actually work for her.

She said that if there's a white line on the bottom or top of a ticket there's a good chance that it’s a winner.

Richard Lustig is the author of the book Learn How To Increase Your Chances of Winning the Lottery.

Mr Lustig, who passed in 2018, told ABC in 2010 that using winnings to buy additional tickets is a major factor to winning.

“Most people buy a $1 ticket and win $10 and they put the $10 in their pocket. Those people are playing the game wrong," Mr Lustig told ABC.

He suggests that those who win $10 should buy $11 worth of tickets.

He claimed this method won him $98,000 in Florida's Fantasy 5 game.

Other simple strategies:

  • Avoid low numbers
  • Don't choose consecutive numbers
  • Play less popular games
