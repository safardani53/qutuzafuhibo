I bought a dupe for the viral Bottega padded bag - its 1.5k cheaper, looks better &amp; the quality

A HEFTY price tag doesn't always translate into high quality - and one shopper learnt this the hard way.

When it comes to all things fashion, sometimes it's best to invest that little extra, as brands, such as Shein, are notorious for the poor quality.

But does that mean you should be rushing to fork out a fortune to grab designer goods?

Well, as style enthusiast Blair Cooley proved, high-end fashion doesn't necessarily mean good quality, as she took to TikTok to compare a padded Bottega Veneta bag versus a dupe from Amazon.

Showing the condition of the real one, which she claimed to have had only for two months, Blaire said there were already noticeable signs of wear.

''I just think this material isn't great,'' the shopper was honest.
