15 Gemini Season Memes to Wish Your Favorite Air Sign a Happy Birthday

Gemini season is upon us, and we don't even have to tell you it's the best season of the whole zodiac. Here are the best memes to celebrate May and June, or to send to your favorite Gemini on their birthday.

Not to alarm anyone, but the best zodiac season of the year starts on May 21. Of course, I'm talking about Gemini season, that divine time of year that stretches from May 21 to June 2. When you think of Geminis, you might think of very adaptable and gregarious people with a bit of a dual personality. 

That's because Geminis are characterized by the twin emoji, meaning Geminis "contain multitudes," to quote Walt Whitman, a fellow Gemini poet. What that means for you depends on your sign and compatibility with this summer air sign, but one thing's for certain no matter when you were born: You'll never be bored when you're around a Gemini.

So whether it's your season and you want to celebrate with extremely relatable memes, or whether you want to wish a friend or lover a happy birthday and show them you get them, take a scroll through these funny memes that will have any Gemini saying, "If this ain't me, tho."
