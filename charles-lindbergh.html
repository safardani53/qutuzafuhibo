Who is Charles Lindbergh:

Charles Lindbergh is a famous Pilot. He was born on February 4, 1902 and his birthplace is Detroit, MI. Charles is also well known as, Famous aviator and inventor who made the first solo airplane flight across the Atlantic in 1927. The historic flight began in Long Island and ended in Paris.

Charles is originated from United States. He was TIME magazine’s first Person of the Year. Mahatma Gandhi earned the same honor three years later.
