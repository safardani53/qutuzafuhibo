Counting On star and Duggar friend Sierra Jo Dominguez reveals she was 'NOT a virgin' before marriag

Counting On star and Duggar family friend Sierra Jo Dominguez has admitted she was NOT a virgin before marrying husband Mark.

The devout fundamentalist Christian said that while she had 'experience' before getting married, her other half WAS a virgin before they exchanged vows.

Sierra, famous for being the party planner for the Duggars on TLC's 19 Kids and Counting, made the revelation during an online Q&A.

Sierra, 31, asked: "What do you assume about me? (I'll let you know if you're right, or not!)"

One fan replied: "That you were a virgin when you got married"

Posting a photo of her smooching with her pastor husband, Sierra revealed: "Nope. But he was. It's one of the many things I admire about him, and one of the many things he never held against me."

Sierra met Mark when she was 18. He was a customer at the video store where she worked.

They become friends and after six months of hanging out, Mark told Sierra of his feelings for her.

The pair wed on December 20, 2008 and had their first child, Khai David, in 2010. The next year sibling Daylon Gabriel came along.

In 2013 Sierra gave birth to the couple's third child Asher Benjamin. The following year Ellyn Joy arrived.

Towards the end of 2015 Edyn Grace made her appearance and at the end of 2017 Sierra gave birth to Brooks Zechariah.

Seventh child Merrick Zion arrived at the end of September 2019.

With seven children, the couple may give the Duggars a run for their money in the future.

Sierra's bombshell may be enough to get some blushing, but those who know Sierra well realize she had a life before finding God.

Prior to becoming a devout Baptist, Sierra was raised in a regular family who celebrated events such as Halloween and wore bikinis and shorts.

But now she prefers to wear long flowing dresses and skirts like the Duggar matriarch Michelle.

And also like Michelle, at her current rate she could end up with 19 kids by the time she's 50.
