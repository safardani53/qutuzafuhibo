Who is George Springer:

George Springer is a famous Baseball Player. He was born on September 19, 1989 and his birthplace is New Britain, CT. George is also well known as, Outfielder who was named World Series MVP after leading the Houston Astros to the franchise’s first ever championship in 2017. He was drafted by the Astros in the first round of the 2011 MLB Draft. 

George is originated from United States. He joined All-Star second baseman Jose Altuve in Houston in 2014.
