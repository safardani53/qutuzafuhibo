Did Shania Twain Get Fillers &amp; Breast Implants Before DWTS Appearance? Hollywood Life

“Shania Twain is and has been a classic beauty over the years and she has maintained over time. She’s most likely using the latest technology, as Shania‘s face looks refreshed and youthful. She possibly used either radio-frequency or fractional lasers to resurface and build collagen in her mid-face and probably had either a fat transfer to her face or fillers,” Beverly Hills plastic surgeon Dr. Payman J. Danielpour tells HollywoodLife.com EXCLUSIVELY. “She has always had a beautiful neck and sculpted jaw line and she continues that here as well, most likely using non-invasive skin tightening such as the forma by in-mode to avoid jowls and keep her defined angle of the jaw.” As we previously told you, Dancing With the Stars fans were shocked by Shania’s appearance during the Oct. 23 episode, when her face looked much different than it has in the past. But her face wasn’t the only thing viewers were fixated on. Shania also flaunted some major cleavage during a performance of her new song, “Soldier“, and fans on Twitter immediately started speculating about possible breast implants. And guess what? Dr. Payman J. Danielpour definitely believes she had some work done on her chest. “As far as her breasts, they appear fuller especially in the upper pole which leads me to believe that she had either a breast augmentation with or without a lift or fat transfer to her breasts,” he says.

As far as how much work Shania may or may not have had done — that’s hard to say. “It is hard to determine the amount of time spent and it depends on whether it’s surgical or non-surgical. But, I would assume that she is on a regular regimen that includes skin care and lasers to keep her face tight, and that would require a visit to a surgeon at least on a monthly or bimonthly basis. If its surgical then a 4-5 hour surgery could address a face lift and a breast augmentation and lift by my partner and I at the same time,” Dr. Payman J. Danielpour further explains.
