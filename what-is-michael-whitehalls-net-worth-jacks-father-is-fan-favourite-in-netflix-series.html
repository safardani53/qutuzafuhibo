Meet Michael Whitehall

Michael is 80 years old and is best-known for being comedian Jack Whitehall’s father who stars alongside him on Netflix’s Jack Whitehall: Travels With My Father. He is a former theatrical agent behind two production companies, Havahall Pictures in 1988, and Whitehall Films in 1998.

During his childhood, he attended Yorkshire boarding school St. Thomas’ House Ampleforth College and went on to have three children Jack, Molly and Barnaby.

Michael has also written several books including Him & Me –  co-written with Jack about their relationship – as well as Backing Into The Spotlight: A Memoir, and Shark Infested Waters: Tales Of An Actors’ Agent. He has previously represented Colin Firth and Dame Judi Dench.
