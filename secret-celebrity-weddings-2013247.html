Niecy Nash and more stars were pros at keeping their wedding ceremonies private.

“Mrs. Carol Denise Betts,” Nash wrote via Instagram in August 2020 alongside a photo from her wedding to singer Jessica Betts. In a separate post on her Instagram Story, the Rookie: Feds star joked, “#PlotTwist.”
