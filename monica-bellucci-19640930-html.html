Monica Bellucci (Movie Actress) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Monica Anna Maria Bellucci is an Italian actress and model. She began her career as a model, model for Dolce & Gabbana and Dior, before moving on to Italian films and then to American and French films. Bellucci played Dracula's bride in Francis Ford Coppola's gothic romance, Francis Ford Coppola's Dracula and Malèna Scordia, in the Italian romantic film Malèna. She starred in the controversial horror film Gaspar Noé Irréversible and played the role of Mary Magdalene in Mel Gibson's biblical film The Passion of the Christ. In the 2003 sci-fi films The Matrix Reloaded and The Matrix Revolutions in 2003, she played Persephone. In the 2015 James Bond film Specter, she became the oldest Bond girl in the series' history.
