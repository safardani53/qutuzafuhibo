Veronika Rajek's most daring outfits of 2023 from tiny bikinis to revealing Las Vegas GP and Europa

INFLUENCER Veronika Rajek has had a crazy year after shooting to fame last December.

The model from Slovakia first turned heads when she watched Tom Brady play and professed her love for him.

Rajek's life looks a lot different now after a year in the spotlight.

Her social media following has continued to grow and she now has more than 6 million followers.

Veronika, 27, has been to a number of events in 2023 and often delights fans with her content and outfit choices.

The day Brady announced his retirement from football earlier this year, she posed in a tiny red bikini and shared a cryptic message about new beginnings.

The Slovakian beauty threw her weight behind the Philadelphia Eagles with her Super Bowl outfit choice in February.

But she was left disappointed as the Kansas City Chiefs came out on top in a 38-35 win.

The model is not shy to show off her amazing body in a tiny bikini.

Some of her most daring this year have included bananas on the front and a mermaid theme.

This year, her outfits have wowed at events like the Coachella festival in Los Angeles and sitting court side at a Miami Heat game.

And she did so by ending the year how 2022 finished - by paying homage to Brady.
