Movie Actress Allison Hayes was born on March 6, 1930 in West Virginia, United States (She dies at the age of 46, on February 27, 1977).

She was most known for her portrayal as a Southern belle in the 1955 picture Count Three and Pray, but she also appeared in the 1958 B-movie Attack of the 50 Foot Woman. Her TV credits include roles in The Rough Riders, Rawhide, and 77 Sunset Strip, among others.
