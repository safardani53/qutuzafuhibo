Danniella Westbrook flashes her pink bra on a night out in London

DANNIELLA Westbrook went for a daring look as she enjoyed a night out with friends in London.

The former EastEnders star flashed her pink bra which was left on show thanks to her plunging jacket.

Danniella co-ordinated her white top with matching skin-tight trousers and accessorised with a designer black belt.

Her bra added a pop of colour to her look as she strutted into the nightclub in a pair of thigh-high black boots.

The star was heading to the Luxx Club in Mayfair for a night out with pals and a chance for her to let her hair down after an intense time as of late which saw her admitted to hospital.

Former Celebrity Big Brother star Danniella was joined by another famous Big Brother alum for the outing, Aisleyne Horgan-Wallace.
