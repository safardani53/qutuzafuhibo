Who Is Naomi Dann? Harvey Barnes Girlfriend Or Wife

The FA Cup winner, Harvey Barnes, and Naomi Dann have been dating for a decade and are still going strong. Naomi Dann is Harvey Barnes’s girlfriend, and the two are yet to get married.

Barnes and Dann are on their way to becoming parents. 

Harvey Lewis Barnes, born on December 9, 1997, in Burnley, Lancashire, England, plays for Leicester City in the EFL Championship.

He was born to his parents, Paul Barnes and Wendy Barnes.

His father, Paul, was a former English soccer player, so it’s only natural that his son, Harvey, possessed athletic traits from a young age.

Barnes came through the Leicester academy and climbed up the ranks to become a Winger for the club. He has been with Leicester since the age of nine.

Furthermore, Harvey signed his first contract with the club on 25 June 2016 and shortly debuted for the first team as a substitute.

With quick feet and the ability to score solo goals, Leicester’s No. 7 has lived up to that expectation, scoring over 30 goals and adding silverware for the Club he joined as a kid.

Together with his boyhood club, Harvey is a one-time FA Cup & Community Shield winner.

Barnes is a talented product of the Club’s Academy, and his performance has caught the interest at both club and International levels, with a move to Newcastle United F.C. now looking feasible.

Personal terms between the club and the player have already been agreed upon, with Leicester set to receive around $42-$43.4 million from Newcastle, as reported by Fabrizio Romano two days ago.

Naomi Dann: Harvey Barnes’s Girlfriend Or Wife

Harvey Barnes and his girlfriend, Naomi Dann, have been in a relationship since 2013. They have not married or engaged yet.

The lovebirds met at school when they were just 13.

The two are on their way to welcoming their first child, a baby girl, breaking out the news on April 16, as shared by Naomi. Similarly, they have a pet dog named Alfie.

Naomi was born on June 12, 1998. Similar to Harvey, his girlfriend Naomi was also born in Burnley.

Unlike her boyfriend, who tirelessly worked to become a professional soccer player, Naomi chose to pursue a degree, and in 2020 she graduated from the university.

After graduation, Naomi followed her passion and entered the influencing scene. 

By following her dreams and remaining true to her passion, today, she is an influencer by profession.

Similarly, the 25-year-old works as a model and influencer for many brands. She is also the brand ambassador of an online fitness brand, InstaFit, which sells sportswear & offers workout tips.

Despite a strong online presence, Dann likes to keep her personal life away from paparazzi lenses. Very limited information is available regarding her family, but we’ve come to know that she has two older sisters, Beth and Emily.

Their love has carried the weight of the test of time as Harvey has broken into and climbed up the Leicester ranks, including England’s first team call up 3 years ago.

A Travel And A Fitness Freak

Barnes’s girlfriend is a fitness enthusiast and a travel freak. She works hard to maintain her body.

As an influencer, she also encourages her fans to live a healthy & quality life.

In addition to this, Naomi is a travel freak and an adventurer. She has been to places like Greece, Spain, UAE, and many more.

Mostly, the couple loves to go out exploring new places during their free time.
