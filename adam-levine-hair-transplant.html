Has Adam Levine Gotten Hair Transplants? Fans Certainly Think So

Does Adam Levine get hair transplants? The Maroon 5 singer is now completely bald, and he showed off his new look in his latest music video.

Every mom's celebrity crush, Adam Levine, just debuted a brand new 'do. The Maroon 5 singer shaved his head entirely, and showed off his completely bald look in a brand-new music video for the song "Nobody's Love." While the 41-year-old heartthrob pulls it off swimmingly, fans of the pop star are shocked, and can't help but wonder why he decided to take the leap and get rid of his luscious locks.

Was Adam Levine going bald anyway? Here's what we know.

Our beloved Adam has sported a number of unique hairstyles within the last two years alone. According to BuzzFeed, prior to the big buzz, he's donned short 'dos, mohawks, bleach blonde beach-y locks, and even braids. Each of these looks worked relatively well for him, so fans were suspicious that he decided to go completely bald... had he been heading toward baldness naturally, and using hair transplants all along?

Rumors alleging that Adam has gotten hair transplant surgery have been circulating the world wide web for years now. According to Hair Transplant Dubai, he had visible signs of hair loss, but suddenly fixed it about six years ago. Fame Watcher also speculated on the matter, pointing out his change in appearance: 

"Levine looked like he was once losing a battle with male pattern baldness, but his hair looks fuller in recent years, and his hairline looks like it may have shifted forward a bit. "

However, according to Belgravia Centre, Adam is bald by choice. Until Adam confirms the rumor himself, we'll never know if he has been getting hair transplants, and now has decided to remove them, but we have our suspicions. Either way, though, he looks seriously fabulous.
