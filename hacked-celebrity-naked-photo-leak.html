What was the big iCloud hack?

On 31 August 31, 2014, a collection of almost 500 private pictures of celebrities were posted on the imageboard 4chan, and later shared by other websites and social networks such as Imgur and Reddit.

The images were believed to have been obtained via a breach of Apple's cloud services suite iCloud, but it later turned out that the hackers could have taken advantage of a security issue in the iCloud API which allowed them to make unlimited attempts at guessing victims' passwords.

The leak prompted concern about the privacy and security of cloud computing services such as iCloud—with a particular emphasis on their use to store sensitive, private information.

What are the most infamous celebrity sex tapes?
