Photos Of The Actor &amp; His 7 Children Hollywood Life

Kevin Costner is one of the 90s most beloved actors but did you know he’s also a huge family man? The star, who is known for his work on The Bodyguard, Field Of Dreams, Yellowstone, and more, is a father of seven, whose daughters include Annie, Lily and Grace, and sons are Joe, Liam, Cayden and Hayes. His oldest and youngest children are a full 26 years apart in age.

Kevin shares his kids with his first wife Cindy Costner, former girlfriend Bridget Rooney, and most recently estranged wife, Christine Baumgartner. Some of his children are in the entertainment industry like their father. Others have opted to stay out of the limelight.

On May 2, 2023, Kevin’s rep confirmed to HollywoodLife that he and Christine have decided to divorce after 18 years of marriage. “It is with great sadness that circumstances beyond his control have transpired which have resulted in Mr. Costner having to participate in a dissolution of marriage action,” his rep said at the time.  “We ask that his, Christine’s and their children’s privacy be respected as they navigate this difficult time.”

Here, Kevin is seen at the Jul. 24, 2008 premiere of his film Swing Vote with Christine, center. His children Lily, Joe, and Annie joined them.
